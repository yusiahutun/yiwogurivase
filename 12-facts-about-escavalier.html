FAQs

1. How can I evolve Karrablast into Escavalier?

To evolve Karrablast into Escavalier, you need to trade it for a Shelmet. Once you have traded with another player or via the GTS (Global Trade System), both Karrablast and Shelmet will evolve during the trade, resulting in an Escavalier and an Accelgor.

2. What is Escavalier’s typing?

Escavalier is a Bug/Steel-type Pokémon. This dual typing gives it unique resistances and weaknesses, making it a formidable opponent in battles. It is resistant to Bug, Grass, Ice, Normal, Psychic, Rock, and Steel-type moves, but it is weak to Fire and Flying-type moves.

3. What are some of Escavalier’s notable moves?

Escavalier can learn a variety of powerful moves, including X-Scissor, Iron Head, Megahorn, and Drill Run. These moves take advantage of its Bug and Steel typing, allowing it to deal significant damage to a wide range of opponents.

4. Can Escavalier Mega Evolve?

No, Escavalier does not have a Mega Evolution. However, it can benefit from holding an item like the Assault Vest or Choice Band to boost its stats and maximize its battle potential.

5. How does Escavalier’s relationship with Shelmet work?

Escavalier has an intriguing connection with Shelmet. To evolve a Karrablast into an Escavalier, it must be traded for a Shelmet. During the trade, both Pokémon undergo a transformation. Karrablast evolves into Escavalier, and Shelmet evolves into Accelgor. This reflects a symbiotic relationship between the two Pokémon species.

16 Times Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra Wore Matching Outfits

August 24, 2024
blog

As far as iconic couples go, Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra are high up on our list. Since the moment they first posed together — at the 2017 Met Gala, before they were technically even a couple — they just seemed so in sync. And in the years following, the two have proved that really is the case, even stepping out in coordinating outfits.

To celebrate their two-year wedding anniversary, the duo was at it again, this time wearing black from head to toe. Aside from the fact that both Jonas and Chopra opted for baggy black hoodies (a must-have staple over the past year), they also completed their looks with a hat and white sneakers.

Sometimes, however, this couple's matchy-matchy style isn't immediately obvious; they'll stick with a theme, such as old Hollywood glamour, rather than wearing pieces that are super similar. But, more often than not, Jonas and Chopra will step out in the same exact color scheme, choosing to wear earth tones or all-white, and causing us to wonder, "Wait a minute...did they plan this?"

While we're not sure if Jonas and Chopra indeed lay out their outfits together, or whether it's just a crazy coincidence because they're that alike, we still find joy in their coordinated couple style. Hopefully they'll keep it coming throughout their marriage!

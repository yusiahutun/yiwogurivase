Lenny Kravitz was struggling after splitting from ex-wife but still 'loves her'

April 17, 2024
blog

Lenny Kravitz still has love for his ex-wife, even though they decided to leave the term of partner behind and move on when they broke up.

Artist Lenny Kravitz met his partner at a New Edition concert back in 1985, and the rest was history. Actress Lisa Bonet became Lenny Kravitz’s muse and inspired the celebrity for his debut album Let Love Rule. She was also behind his new look that came with it, encouraging him to grow out his dreadlocks, which has become a staple for the rock star.

Lenny Kravitz married Lisa Bonet two years later

The American Woman star married Lisa in 1987 and they welcomed daughter Zoë Kravitz the following year.

“We vibed immediately,” he wrote in his 2020 memoir, Let Love Rule. “Time stood still. Without a lot being said, there was magnetism. I’d never had an encounter like it before. We were from the same tribe.”

They tied the knot in Las Vegas in November, 1987, however, despite sparks flying between them when they first met, they separated in 1991 and divorced two years later. But that hasn’t stopped Lenny Kravitz from still having a deep love for his wife and ex-partner.

He said: “Zoë’s mom and I now are best friends. It’s interesting because that’s how the relationship started. It makes you feel really good when you can do that. When we’re all together now.”

But he admits it took a while to get to this stage.

“It took time, believe me, but she is a part of my life that will never leave my heart, my soul or my spirit. I am what I am because of our experience,” he told People earlier this month.

Lenny Kravitz and his ex-wife share a daughter

Lenny and his ex-wife only had one child together, Zoë Kravitz, who has followed in her parents’ footsteps. She starred in Big Little Lies, The Batman as well as X-Men movies.

Although Lenny’s fame began with music, he has made a mark in the film business too. He starred as Cinna in The Hunger Games franchise alongside Jennifer Lawrence.

And the 59-year-old has a strong relationship with his daughter.

“She’s amazing, and she’s all the things that I would have hoped for,” he added.

“I’m a dad, you know, but we’re friends. We’re very close. We talk about everything. We don’t hide things from each other.”

He says their time together was ‘beautiful’

“We were quite the family,” Lenny Kravitz said about his time with ex-wife Lisa and daughter Zoe.

“We had our own groove, expression, fashion. It was a beautiful time. The world Zoë’s mom and I were creating, our family, it was about peace, love and spirit.”

Although they broke up almost 15 years ago, they’re still good friends. He said he was struggling emotionally after they split when they married young, but they worked together to make sure they were still close.

“The love doesn’t leave you, but it has to find a new way to funnel itself into a new life,” he added.

“We consciously wanted to do that. It took time, believe me, but she is a part of my life that will never leave my heart, my soul or my spirit. I am what I am because of our experience.”

Lenny Kravitz list of partners include model and ‘famous movie star’

Lenny, whose parents were also in Hollywood, has had a celebrity-filled love life.

Before Lenny Kravitz married his ex-partner Lisa, he reportedly dated Kylie Minogue for a short spell in 1991. Although the singers never responded to the claims.

The follow year he began dating Vanessa Paradis, between 1992 and 1997, after the French star sought him out to produce her first English album. Although neither of the pair opened up over their split at the time, Lenny did later say he was “madly in love” with the star, but “she came at the wrong time”. He would introduce Paradis to Johnny Depp, and the pair went on to date for 14 years and share two children.

Then in 2002, he was linked to Nicole Kidman, but Lenny Kravitz’ Hollywood partner wasn’t known for almost 15 years later. They even got engaged, but kept it all under wraps, but unfortunately it wasn’t meant to be as they later broke up too.

The movie star said in 2007 they “weren’t ready” to get married, but kept the name hush-hush.

In 2018, he was linked to Brazilian model Barbara Fialho, but it’s believed they called it quits before they reached their first anniversary.

After, rumors flew he was dating Halle Berry, but neither of them confirmed the news, so that seems to live on as the typical speculation that surrounds celebrity couples.

Lisa on the other hand had a long-term relationship with marvel star Jason Momoa. They got together in 2005, married in 2017 and announced they had separated in 2022.

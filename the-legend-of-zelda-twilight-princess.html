Poes - The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess Guide

September 19, 2024
blog

Jovani in Hyrule Castle Town has a crushed spirit, literally, and it's your job to locate the remains. The pieces are being held by 60 Poes around Hyrule. You may have seen the floating lanterns throughout Hyrule - those are being held by the Poes, and the ones outside only appear at night. Use Wolf Link's sense to see them and then attack. After two hits they'll drop to the ground. Press A once more to finish them off and collect its soul. You can also use Midna's energy wave to target the Poe, then hit it, making it fall in one shot.

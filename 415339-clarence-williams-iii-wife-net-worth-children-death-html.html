Clarence Williams III biography

The Mod Squad star was born on August 21st, 1939, in New York City, New York, in the United States. He was the son of Clarence "Clay" Williams Jr., a professional musician. Not much about his mother is in the limelight.

Williams III was raised by his paternal grandparents, Clarence Williams, and his wife, Eva Taylor. He developed a passion for acting after accidentally walking onto a stage at a theatre below a Harlem YMCA.

Profession

Clarence Williams III had an illustrious career working both in film and television series. He began his acting career in 1957, playing a minor in Dark of the Moon. He temporarily stopped acting when he went for his military service.

When he completed his military service, he proceeded with his acting career. In 1960, he appeared in The Long Dream as Chris. He continued to perform in various productions.

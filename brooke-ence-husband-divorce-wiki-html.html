Brooke Ence is a multi-faceted personality. She has her hands dipped in various sectors, such as athletics, social media, and entrepreneurship.

Brooke is a CrossFit athlete and has been taking part in CrossFit games since 2010. She also trains people at the CrossFit West Santa Cruz in California.

As for her entrepreneurial side of things, she owns a shoe brand named NOBULL Trainers; she launched her brand on the eve of the 2015 CrossFit Games. She also has an athleisure clothing line called Encewear, a training app called Naked, and a skincare line called Athia.

Mom found unconscious from overdose with baby in backseat

February 26, 2024
blog

A 24-year-old woman from New Jersey was found slumped behind the wheel of her car after overdosing on heroin while her three-month-old son cried in the back seat.

Katelyn Cox was unconscious in the driver's seat of her Hyundai Sonata which she had left running outside a bagel shop and pizzeria in Staten island, New York.

Cox only regained consciousness after EMS workers gave her naloxone, an anti-overdose spray.

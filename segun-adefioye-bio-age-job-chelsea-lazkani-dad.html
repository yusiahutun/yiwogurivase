Segun Adefioye Job

According to Segun Adefioye’s LinkedIn, he started his career as an architect at NICKSON & BORYS and PARTNERS IKOYI LAGOS. He joined the company in 1984 and worked in the firm until 1989. During that period, he played a pivotal role in several projects such as “NECOM HOUSE(NITEL) RESTORATION & COCOA HOUSE RESTORATION & other projects at various stages of construction which include 12 MARINA, LAGOS, 23 MARINA, LAGOS & 46 MARINA, LAGOS”.

After that, Segun moved to London and joined Seifert Architects in 1989. He worked for the firm as an architect till 1991 and worked in PRODUCTION DRAWINGS for several projects in London which include 10-12 Great Marlborough St, London, and W1 & ROSE COURT, Southwark Bridge Road, London SE 1.

Starting from 1990, Segun has been serving as a principal consultant at Planning & Property Solutions. He has been working in the company for over 32 years. In September 2012, he also worked for Bespoke Interiors as a consultant and has also been working for that company ever since.

As for his education, Segun graduated from King’s College in 1977, Ahmadu Bello University in 1984 with an M.Sc. in Architecture, and the University of Westminster with a Diploma in Town Planning.

Gillian Anderson's Golden Globes dress featured vaginas with saucy G-spot hint

Gillian Anderson wore a dress covered in vaginas to the Golden Globes.

At the star-studded event, Gillian wore a strapless gown that showed a lot of skin on her top half as she posed on the red carpet. The upper part of her gown cinched her in at the waist and clung to her figure, and the actress accessorised with a diamond necklace, matching earrings and circular silver bag.

On the lower half of Gillian’s dress, the skirt billowed out down to the floor behind her as she walked and posed for the cameras. Hidden on the dress was a number of embroidered “yonis” to support Anderson’s sexual wellness brand.

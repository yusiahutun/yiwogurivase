    By Amy de Klerk

    Celine embraced a little Hollywood glitz and glamour as it hosted its latest catwalk show at the Wiltern Theatre in Los Angeles where Hedi Slimane presented a series of sequin gowns that were certainly befitting of the setting. Entitled 'The Age of Indieness', this was the autumn/winter 2023 collection from the house.

    And, a Hollywood setting called for an A-list front row. Cindy Crawford, Courteney Cox, Brie Larson and Emma Roberts were among the stars to appear, while the likes of Kaia Gerber and Dree Hemingway took to the catwalk.

Ubiq (UBQ) is an open-source blockchain platform.

Ubiq is a decentralized platform that allows developers to build and deploy smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps). It is based on the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM), making it compatible with existing Ethereum tools and infrastructure.

Ubiq was launched in 2017.

Ubiq was introduced to the cryptocurrency market in January 2017, with the aim of providing a secure and stable blockchain platform for businesses and developers.

Ubiq utilizes its native cryptocurrency, UBQ.

The UBQ token is used as the primary utility and governance token on the Ubiq network. It is used for transaction fees, staking, and participating in network consensus.

Ubiq uses Proof-of-Work (PoW) consensus algorithm.

Similar to Bitcoin, Ubiq relies on miners to validate transactions and secure the network through the PoW algorithm. This ensures the integrity and immutability of the blockchain.

Ubiq offers enhanced security through merge mining.

Ubiq is merge-mined with Ethereum, which means that miners can simultaneously mine both Ubiq and Ethereum, providing increased security for the Ubiq network.

Ubiq has a supply limit of 40 million tokens.

Unlike some other blockchain platforms, Ubiq has a finite supply of UBQ tokens, with a maximum limit of 40 million tokens. This helps to maintain scarcity and potentially increase the token’s value over time.

Ubiq focuses on providing stability and long-term support.

Ubiq aims to differentiate itself by prioritizing stability and offering long-term developer support. This ensures that businesses and developers can build on a reliable and sustainable platform.

Ubiq offers a unique decentralized governance system.

Ubiq has implemented a decentralized governance system called Ubiquity. It allows UBQ token holders to vote on proposals and participate in the decision-making process for platform upgrades and improvements.

Ubiq has a strong and active community.

The Ubiq community is passionate and actively involved in the development and growth of the platform. There are forums, social media channels, and developer resources available for users to connect and collaborate.

2.         PRIZE: The prize of the Contest consists of the following:  (a) two (2) tickets for a Blake Shelton concert on his upcoming U.S. tour. Selection of concert date/location from tour schedule is in Sponsor’s discretion, and location/date will be communicated to winner at time of winner selection/validation; (b) round-trip airfare from the nearest U.S. gateway airport to the winner’s primary home in the 48 contiguous United States to the city of the concert in coach class (or equivalent) for the winner and one (1) guest on a carrier and on dates to be specified by Sponsor; (c) two (2) nights hotel accommodations (room reservation and tax fees only) for the winner and one guest in a hotel and on dates to be determined by Sponsor; (d) opportunity to winner and one (1) guest to participate in a meet and greet with Blake Shelton (subject to Artist’s availability).

The winner will be notified by mail, e-mail or telephone.  In the event that Sponsor cannot reach the winner within three (3) days after it attempts to notify the winner, Sponsor will select a new winner according to these rules. Additionally, if the winner is ineligible or cannot attend concert on the dates specified, it will be deemed as if the winner has rejected the prize, and Sponsor will select a new winner.

To claim the Prize, the winner must:

(a)        confirm receipt of the notice by return email or telephone call; and

(b)       complete, sign and return a Winner Affidavit & Release form (to be provided by Sponsor), a 1099-MISC IRS tax form (to be provided by Sponsor) and such other documentation (if any) requested by Sponsor.

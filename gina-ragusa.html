Gina Ragusa is a veteran of the journalism industry, covering all aspects of entertainment, which includes comedians, musicians, and reality TV. She is an expert on Bravo series including Below Deck and its extensive cast members, The Real Housewives, and Vanderpump Rules. She’s trusted by many readers to provide a true look behind the scenes at the drama of reality TV, but also into real issues, like new relationships, diversity and inclusion among the casts, and overcoming addiction.

Gina’s exclusive interviews give readers a chance to know more about the humanity and essence of the people she speaks with, which helps her to build a unique story every time. Over her career, she’s built a network of insider entertainment sources and connections with industry professionals that provide her with unique insights into breaking news. Read more of her work featuring Shaquille O’Neal, Bastille, Shaggy, Jesse Tyler Ferguson, Alison Brie, and Derek Hough, to name several.

She admires dark satirists like John Mullaney, David Sedaris, Augusten Burroughs, and Jerry Stahl. See Gina’s previous work on TheStreet, Vice, Mic, and Business Insider, along with Muck Rack.

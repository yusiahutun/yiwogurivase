Zo Kravitz and Channing Tatum's Full Relationship Timeline

July 20, 2024
blog

After two years together, Zoë Kravitz and Channing Tatum are stronger than ever.

In a love story that begins with Tatum as a muse, Kravitz and the Magic Mike star first connected back in 2017, when the Batman actor personally scouted him to star as Slater King in her directorial debut and thriller, Blink Twice (formerly known as Pussy Island). After working together on the film, the two got close and friendly on various New York City dates, and ultimately fell into a romantic relationship by 2021.

Now engaged, the pair continue to be spotted on PDA-filled outings around the world and frequently gush about one another in interviews, on both a personal and a professional level.

Ahead, all the highlights from Tatum and Kravitz’s sweet relationship.

March 20, 2024: Lenny talks about his relationship with Channing.

During an interview with Sherri Shepherd, Lenny opens up about his relationship with his future son-in-law.

“He’s a great guy. We get on really well,” he says. “We have our own relationship as well. We hang out. We talk. He’s a very soulful human being. He was raised well. He has manners and class.”

Of his first meeting with the 21 Jump Street star, Kravitz says it was “very natural. Like I said, he’s very sweet. It works. Life is about timing and what you’ve gone through, what you’ve learned, and they’re in the right place.”

March 12, 2024: Zoë and Channing attend Lenny's Hollywood Walk of Fame star ceremony.

Tatum accompanies his fiancée to Lenny Kravitz’s Hollywood Walk of Fame star ceremony in Hollywood. The couple look so in love while sitting together at the event for her rock-star dad.

January 30, 2024: Lenny Kravitz says the couple’s love is “beautiful to watch.”

The rock star tells People in his new cover story that his daughter’s relationship with fiancé Tatum just “feels right.” Says Kravitz, “I like [Channing] very much.”

The couple, Lenny says, “have something that’s naturally special.” But more importantly, “they also do the work. They are dedicated. That's what it’s all about. It’s beautiful to watch.” He further explains: “It has to be something beyond that love, and that is dedication. So that is something that I see that they have.”

Lenny, who shares Zoë with ex-wife Lisa Bonet, adds that he likes the Magic Mike actor “very much.” “It’s wonderful to welcome him to the family,” he says.

Kravitz indulged her 7.9 million Instagram followers by sharing a rare photo of herself and Tatum. The Polaroid snap shows Tatum smoldering at the camera in an unbuttoned flowy white button-down, matching shorts, and a pale blue bucket hat. Kravitz stands beside him, pulling her face mask down to her neck and sticking her tongue out, wearing a loose-fitting yellow, red, blue, and white striped button-down over a black bra, red shorts, and a black baseball cap. The lovebirds pose on what appears to be a movie set.

“#BLINKTWICE in theaters august 23rd 🌴,” she captioned the post. The long-awaited thriller film is, of course, instrumental to how the pair’s relationship started. They first connected when Kravitz sent Tatum a draft of the script and successfully recruited him to star as the lead.

January 4, 2024: Lenny Kravitz discusses the engagement news.

“Hey, man. That’s life,” Zoë Kravitz’s musician father, Lenny Kravitz, tells Entertainment Tonight when asked about his daughter and Tatum’s engagement at the Palm Springs International Film Festival.

“When your family is happy, when your child is happy, that’s it—I’ve done well,” he says. “Blessed.”

Kravitz and Tatum are photographed leaving a dinner date at Los Angeles’s Giorgio Baldi. The newly engaged couple share sweet, cuddly moments walking arm in arm, the Batman actor flashing her massive, sparkly, emerald-cut engagement ring.

Sources confirm to People that Kravitz and Tatum, after dating for two years, are officially engaged. Tatum admits to having followed multiple Zöe Kravitz fan accounts on Instagram during a cover-story interview with Vanity Fair. “I have no chill,” the actor explains. “I was just seeing what she was up to! Also, I didn’t know anyone would know.”

November 15, 2022: Zoë reveals the sweet reason she fell in love with Channing.

During an interview with GQ, Kravitz speaks candidly about her romance with Tatum.

“He’s just a wonderful human. He makes me laugh and we both really love art and talking about art and the exploration of why we do what we do. We love to watch a film and break it down and talk about it and challenge each other,” she says.

Working with her on the set of Pussy Island, she says, Tatum proved to be a supportive force. “Whether it was making me tea or pouring me a drink or going to whip someone into shape or whatever—he really was my protector and it was really wonderful and sweet,” Kravitz says. “I think if you can do something like that together, it’s a good test. And we came out even stronger.”

She also reflects on the moment their relationship became public, when a paparazzi snapped the now-viral photo of Kravitz riding on Tatum’s bike. The actor had accompanied Kravitz on a walk to her writing partner’s house. “I was sweating and he was like, ‘Get on the bike, I’ll ride you over and you can relax,’” she recalled. She notes that, almost immediately after that, she saw a pap from the corner of her eye.

“You want to keep it sacred and private as long as you can,” Kravitz says. “So that you don’t have to even think about what the world thinks about it.”

The two celebrate their first year of romance together with a couple’s getaway to Italy, where they are spotted matching in white outfits and kissing on a yacht. They also bring along Everly Tatum, the actor’s nine-year-old daughter from his previous marriage to Jenna Dewan.

August 16, 2022: Zoë talks about working with Channing.

The actor and director discloses more details about working with Tatum on Pussy Island in an interview with WSJ. Magazine, during which she praises his performance in the film and happily recalls her creative experience with him on set.

“When you make things with people, it’s a very sacred space, and when you’re compatible with somebody creatively, it often opens up other channels, because you’re kind of sharing all of yourself,” Kravitz says. She adds: “I’m really grateful that this movie has brought him into my life that way.”

“I felt, even from afar, before I knew him, that he was a feminist and that he wasn’t afraid of exploring that darkness, because he knows he’s not that,” Kravitz continues. “That’s why I was drawn to him and wanted to meet with him. And I was right.”

She also likes that Tatum had yet to play a dark character before this role. “I think that’s exciting to watch someone who’s mostly played boy next door, good guy, love interest, all of that,” she says.

February 14, 2022: Zoë opens up about Channing.

Kravitz praises Tatum for his role as Slater King in Pussy Island in a cover story for Elle. Discussing what drove her decision to cast him in the darker role, she explains that Tatum is so different from the toxic qualities associated with his character, so portraying them onscreen never felt problematic or overly real.

“Looking at his work and hearing him speak about Magic Mike and the live show, I’m like, I think he’s a feminist,” Kravitz says. “You need to be so far from who this is, where it’s not scary. And I don’t think we’ve ever seen him play someone dark. I’m excited to see him do that.”

February 2, 2022: Channing speaks highly of Zoë.

In a cover story for Variety, Tatum opens up about his work with Kravitz on Pussy Island and openly admires her ambitions for the project. “She’s a perfectionist in the best possible way,” he tells the magazine.

Tatum also reveals that Kravitz and his 21 Jump Street costar Jonah Hill have influenced his personal style. “Some friends of mine, like you and [Zoë Kravitz] were like, ‘Why don't you just wear what you wear? Why don’t you just be you?’” Tatum tells Hill in a VMan interview he jointly conducts with his costar. “You gave me some very specific style tips.”

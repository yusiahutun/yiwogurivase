He later attended Shawnee Mission North High School and transferred to the University of Tulsa. From an early age, Phil was gifted in sports and got the chance to feature in his high school football team as a backline.

He was granted a football scholarship at the University of Tulsa, where he switched up his play position to a middle linebacker until he transferred to Midwestern State University.

His father was an oil field equipment supplier before becoming a psychologist, and Dr Phil would follow in his footsteps to achieve the fete. He would graduate with a B.A in psychology in 1976 and a PhD from the University of North Texas in 1979.

Ina Garten Gushes Over Friend Jennifer Garner Dancing to Greases We Go Together

May 08, 2024
blog

A great friend. Ina Garten couldn’t help but gush over Jennifer Garner‘s performance when the actress shared a video on Instagram of her and pro ballerina Tiler Peck dancing to Grease‘s “We Go Together.”

“Wow!! Love Tiler Peck!! And love you too!” Ina, 72, recently commented on the Instagram post with three red-heart emojis. Jennifer, 48, captioned it, “Until @tilerpeck is back where she belongs — on stage with @nycballet — I’ll enjoy the silver lining of having her here in California.”

However, Ina wasn’t the only one who thought Jen did a good job. Actress Zoe Saldana said, “You are so amazing,” and Gisele Bündchen shared three clapping hand emojis in the comments section.

It looks like Alias star is in good spirits after her breakup from ex-boyfriend John Miller. The former couple split in August after less than two years of dating.

“They split up before L.A. went into lockdown,” a source exclusively told In Touch Weekly, who was the first to break the news. “Jen and John remain on friendly terms so there’s a possibility that they could rekindle their relationship in the future, but right now it’s over.”

As Jen takes a break from her love life, she can count on her kids — Violet, Seraphina and Samuel — to keep her busy in quarantine. So far, they’ve been taking day trips to the beach and going on quiet strolls around their neighborhood. In fact, that’s the reason why Jennifer bought a cat stroller in the first place.

“She doesn’t want the children being glued to their iPads or playing computer games all day,” another insider told Closer in March about the actress’ three kids whom she shares with ex-husband Ben Affleck. “So to keep them busy, they’ll bake and decorate cakes together — the kitchen is a complete mess afterward, but she doesn’t care.”

In quarantine, the Daredevil star has also been homeschooling her kids and dressing in the funniest fashions at home, which she likes to call “lockdown fierce.” Seems like Jen is really keeping herself busy during this tough time in America.

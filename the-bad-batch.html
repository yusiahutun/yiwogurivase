What happened to the Clone Army after the Revenge of the Sith? Star Wars: The Bad Batch follows a group of elite clones with genetic mutations and special skills (all voiced by Dee Bradley Baker) who strike out on their own as mercenaries during the Empire’s early days. Not unlike Andor, the show deals with some of the thornier issues and harsh realities of fascism and the Star Wars universe, albeit in a slightly more kid-friendly way.

Season one of The Bad Batch premiered on Disney+ in 2021. The second season began in early 2023.

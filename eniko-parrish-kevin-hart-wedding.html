Eniko Parrish and Kevin Hart's Wedding

March 18, 2024
blog

Eniko Parrish wore not one, but two custom Vera Wang bridal gowns to marry Kevin Hart this past weekend in Santa Barbara, California. The couple said their vows in front of family and friends after seven years together. Kevin's children, Hendrix (who served as Hart's best man) and Heaven were both key members in their bridal party.

Parrish donned two custom Vera Wang gowns for the event, wearing a soft-white mermaid gown with a tulle skirt and sheer lace V-neck bodice with Chantilly lace appliqués down the aisle paired with a complementing veil. For the reception, the bride wore a long sleeve sheer lace gown with hand-placed Chantilly lace appliqués. Parrish shared her all of her looks, and her excitement about finally being #MrsHart, on her Instagram yesterday.

For those looking to score a similar look(s), Vera Wang has introduced styles like Parrish's custom gowns in the past. Ana Luisa, a sheer lace long sleeve fit-and-flare silhouette, bears striking resemblance to Parrish's reception gown, while her ceremony dress looks like it could have been a combination of two of Vera Wang's Spring 2016 styles, Daniela and Amalia.

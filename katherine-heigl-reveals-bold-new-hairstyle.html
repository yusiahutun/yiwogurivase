Katherine Heigl reveals bold new hairstyle

May 07, 2024
blog

Katherine Heigl is well known for her long, platinum blonde tresses, but now the 37-year-old has swapped her trademark look for a dramatic new style. With a punky brunette undercut and blonde highlights, the star looked completely different as she hung out with friends earlier this week.

Posing for the camera in a simple yet stylish grey jumper dress and dangly earrings, the former Grey's Anatomy star debuted her new do as she partied with two friends at the Café Stella in L.A. The star was also spotted as she arrived at LAX airport with her new style loose and without any product for travelling.

Fans complimented her on her new look

Although the actress looked delighted by her new style, she didn't acknowledge her do, captioning the photo: "First order of business in LA...girls night at Cafe Stella with my gorgeous gal pals @cheyenneellis and @irritated_one. Love you guys like crazy! Only thing that would have made the night total perfection @tawnylehman sitting there with us!"

BLONDE VS BRUNETTE: CELEBRITY HAIR TRANSFORMATIONS

Fans were quick to compliment the mother-of-two on the new brunette look. One Instagram user wrote: "your hair looks amazing!" while another gushed: "You cut your hair??? You look stunning!!!"

Katherine isn't the only star to radically change their look recently, as Alesha Dixon unveiled a gorgeous new look earlier this month after cutting her hair into a bob and dying it bright purple.

THE BEST CELEBRITY BOBS

Speaking about the decision to have her hair cut short, the 37-year-old told HELLO! Online: "You know what it's like, you just get bored. I fancied a change and was like, 'New Year, tenth year, chop it off.' I didn't realise it was gonna go so short but my boyfriend has been telling me to chop it off for ages."

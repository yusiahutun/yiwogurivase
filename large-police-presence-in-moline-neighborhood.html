UPDATE: Police investigate suspicious deaths in Moline; ask for publics help

July 25, 2024
blog

UPDATE: Moline police are investigating two suspicious deaths, according to a news release from Moline Police.

Law enforcement has been at a home on the 1800 block of 46th Street since about 10:30 a.m. Wednesday. Police say they went to that home for a medical problem and found a deceased woman. Illinois State Police crime scene investigators and Moline police remained on the scene Wednesday.

Our Quad Cities News crews saw police continue to go in and out of the house and take a lot of pictures inside.

Earlier at about 9:40 a.m., police responded to a similar call on the 3300 block of 25th Avenue for a medical problem, where they found a deceased male. Both deaths are under investigation.

The Moline Police Department Criminal Investigation Division is investigating both deaths and being assisted by the Rock Island County Sheriff’s Office and Illinois State Police. Police ask anyone with information to call Moline Police at 309-797-0401 or anonymously through Crime Stoppers at 309-762-9500, or through the P3 Tips App.

No other information was available Wednesday evening.

EARLIER: A large police presence is outside of a home in Moline.

Illinois State Police and the Moline Police Department are on 46th street in a neighborhood north of Avenue of the Cities. Crime scene tape is in front of the home. Our Quad Cities News saw officers going in and out of the home, and investigators aren’t saying anything about the case.

Our Quad Cities News will have more information as it becomes available.

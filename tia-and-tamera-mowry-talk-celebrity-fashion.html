Drab or Fab!? Tia and Tamera Mowry Talk Celebrity Fashion!

August 17, 2024
blog

Tia Mowry-Hardrict and Tamera Mowry-Housley are all grown up!

The adorable teenage girls we grew to love on the hit series, "Sister, Sister," are now television veterans. Tia currently stars in "Instant Mom," and Tamera is a daytime talk show co-host on "The Real."

The twins have been looking glamorous on the red carpet for years, so toofab's Rachel Hoffman caught up with the ladies -- during a press day for the Let's Bubble campaign created by Scrubbing Bubbles -- to dish about celebrity fashion.

Who got their vote for "drab" or "fab?" Check out the video above to see what Tia and Tamera had to say about questionable outfits worn by Paris Hilton, Jenna Dewan-Tatum, Emma Roberts, Jennifer Hudson and more!

Jeff Bezos' fiancee insists that the statue on his boat is not based on her because she lacks breast

March 07, 2024
blog

Lauren Sanchez, Jeff Bezos' fiancee, once again stressed that the bust on her husband's boat is not a representation of her, as she lacks breasts, she jokingly pointed out. The founder of Amazon presented this year his yacht 'love boat' of 416 feet, which is the tallest sailboat in the world.

Something that did not go unnoticed was the bust that appears at the tip of the boat, a yacht worth 500 million dollars and that is crowned with that sculpture that has a great resemblance to his wife. But Lauren pointed out in Vogue that it is not her.

A curvy woman with a flowing dress and long hair can be seen, making it appear to be Lauren: "I'm very flattered, but it's not," she said.

Instead, she divulged that the sculpture was based on one of her fiance's favorite mythological figures - Freyja, the Norse goddess of love, fertility, war and gold. "If it were me...," she emphasized as she made the plus-breasted gesture.

What is the millionaires' bunker?

Apart from the sailboat, the tycoon is also involved in the 'millionaires' bunker' project. These are subway shelters that are being built by wealthy people to protect themselves from a possible catastrophe, such as a nuclear war, a natural disaster or a pandemic. The name is Indian Creek Village and the island bears the same name, Indian Creek Island, located in Biscayne Bay, 12 km from Miami's financial center.

These shelters are usually equipped with everything needed to survive for an extended period of time, including food, water, medicine, weapons and ammunition, as well as security and communication systems.

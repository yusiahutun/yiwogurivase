Sip Tequila Hosts Grand Opening Event

May 04, 2024
blog

Sip Tequila in Stuart will host the First Annual Sip Tequila Fest & Grand Opening to celebrate the debut of its new retail experience on September 30, from 1 to 5 p.m.

Tequila enthusiasts are invited to celebrate a day of tequila and agave education, featuring more than 50 premium tequilas to taste, exciting giveaways every 30 minutes, live education sessions, delectable food options, and much more.

The event will honor the launch of Sip Tequila’s new retail experience and storefront at 2210 SE Federal Highway in Stuart. Designed to provide an innovative spirits shopping experience, the space is perfect for picking out a new favorite tequila expression or booking a private tasting for a group of friends.

Beneath an air-conditioned tent, guests will have the opportunity to savor more than 50 premium tequilas and mezcals from renowned distilleries across Mexico. From crisp blanco to rich extra añejo to vibrant wild agave mezcal, the Grand Opening Tasting will cater to both seasoned aficionados and those eager to explore the world of tequila and other agave spirits. All attendees will also get an automatic discount on all purchases made the same day.

Every half hour, Sip Tequila will host giveaways of exclusive tequila bottles, Sip Tequila merchandise and gift cards, surprises from participating vendors, and a grand prize of a catered private tasting in their brand new Sip Tequila Lounge. The excitement will continue with free education sessions presented by industry experts, distilling icons, and influencers.

General admission tickets are $55 and VIP tickets are $200. The event is for adults aged 21 and older; no children, babies, or pets will be granted entry. For more information and to get tickets, head to siptequila.com.

This member of La Roche-Posay's Hyalu family has an especially interesting formulation, combining the humectant benefits of hyaluronic acid with broad-spectrum SPF and protective antioxidants.

The sensory treat

Fresh Rose Deep Hydration Sleeping Mask

The sensory treat

Fresh Rose Deep Hydration Sleeping Mask

It's true that most hyaluronic-acid based products are marketed from a clinical, fun-free angle, but if you prefer your skincare to have sensorial appeal, there are options on the table. Our top recommendation would be this intensive overnight mask from Fresh: the brand that does rose-based skincare better than anyone else.

The dual-phase product combines a humectant gel formula (rich in damask rose extracts and hyaluronic acid) alongside an emollient cream with which to lock in all the moisture.

The collagen booster

Medik8 Liquid Peptides™

The collagen booster

Medik8 Liquid Peptides™

There is a classic hyaluronic acid serum in Medik8's line-up (the Hydr8 B5), but we think this supercharged option works harder. It contains low-weight HA alongside several high-performing peptides, all of which boost the skin's own collagen production.

The redness reliever

SkinCeuticals Phyto Corrective Essence Mist

The redness reliever

SkinCeuticals Phyto Corrective Essence Mist

Now 14% Off

If you're got sensitive skin, you've likely come across SkinCeuticals' relied-upon Phyto Corrective collection before. (The original Phyto Corrective Gel can bring down redness like no other.)

Now, there's a soothing face mist to add to your anti-inflammatory line-up. More than a simple refreshing spritz, this thoughtfully formulated product combines hyaluronic acid with calming botanicals: think rosemary and cucumber. It'll take the heat out of reactive skin, bring down inflammation, and rehydrate your barrier. Use it both immediately after cleansing and throughout the day.

The intensive mask

111 Skin Molecular Hydration Mask

The intensive mask

111 Skin Molecular Hydration Mask

They might get eclipsed by those social-media dominating sheet masks, but 111 Skin's leave-on treatments shouldn't be slept on.

There's one for every skin concern, from breakouts to dullness, but for a dose of mega hydration, this one is the winner. Multiple-weight hyaluronic acid leads a line-up of moisture-grabbing humectants, while vitamin C is working in the background to brighten and protect. Apply it overnight to wake up with the juiciest complexion.

The eye treatment

L'Oréal Paris Hyaluronic Acid Eye Serum

The eye treatment

L'Oréal Paris Hyaluronic Acid Eye Serum

Now 16% Off

A watery eye serum is ideal for anyone who finds creams too heavy (or is prone to milia). This brilliantly priced option contains a high concentration of hyaluronic acid, yet is lightweight enough to sink straight into delicate skin, leaving behind no sticky residue.

The rollerball applicator is a welcome addition on tired and puffy eyes, while the cooling texture will leave you feeling refreshed.

The cult investment

Dr. Barbara Sturm Hyaluronic Serum

The cult investment

Dr. Barbara Sturm Hyaluronic Serum

Yes, this one's a weightier investment than most, but Dr. Barbara Sturm's cult Hyaluronic Serum is a facialist favourite for good reason.

With a texture only slightly thicker than water, the formula absorbs instantly, delivering visible results as well as longer-term hydration benefits. Dr. Sturm's entire ethos is focused on reducing inflammation in the skin, so you know this serum will be calming and fortifying on all skin types.

To get the most out of it, apply to still-damp skin after cleansing, then seal in the moisture-boosting benefits with an occlusive cream or face oil.

The smart self-tanner

Tan-Luxe Super Glow Hyaluronic Self Tan Serum

The smart self-tanner

Tan-Luxe Super Glow Hyaluronic Self Tan Serum

Even the most tan-averse should pay attention to this one: Tan-Luxe’s smart serum is essentially a quenching hyaluronic serum with a drop of gentle fake tan.

Massage a few drops directly onto your skin before your night cream to wake up with a bright, even bronze – and plumper cheeks. There's now an SPF 30 iteration for daytime too.

The affordable serum

The affordable serum

CeraVe Hydrating Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Hyaluronic acid serums don't need to be expensive to be effective. Cerave's affordable option won't solve your every skin concern, but it is a great hydrator that can be massaged in with abandon.

If you're oily, acne-prone or in any way nervous about introducing new products into your regime, try this one.

The entry-level hydrator

Vichy Minéral 89 Hyaluronic Acid Hydrating Serum

The entry-level hydrator

Vichy Minéral 89 Hyaluronic Acid Hydrating Serum

This is a great daily hyaluronic acid serum for anyone who simply wants to boost moisture content and keep reaction-prone skin calm and content.

The all-rounder

The all-rounder

Sarah Chapman Skinesis Intense Hydrating Booster

From facialist Sarah Champan's beloved namesake brand, this hyaluronic-powered serum works brilliantly to plump and smooth dry, dehydrated skin. The rosewater base delivers soothing benefits (and a lovely scent), while the hyaluronic acid is cocktailed with barrier-boosting prebiotics to keep your complexion calm and balanced.

The body treatment

Necessaire The Body Retinol

The body treatment

Necessaire The Body Retinol

If you're diligent with a multi-step facial skincare routine but neglecting anything further south, you're really missing a trick. Necessaire's potent body treatment contains three hero ingredients – retinol, alpha-hydroxy-acids and hyaluronic acid – to resurface and rehydrate beyond the neckline. Expect a brighter décolletage, softer elbows, and even a reduction in body breakouts and stretch marks.

The make-up perfector

REN Perfect Canvas Smooth, Prep & Plump Essence

The make-up perfector

REN Perfect Canvas Smooth, Prep & Plump Essence

Much more than a simple hydrator, REN's refreshingly light serum works to hydrate, soothe and strengthen skin with regular use. Hyaluronic acid is partnered with another power humectant, polyglutamic acid, while fermented extracts feed the skin's microbiome.

The pre-party mask

Decree SOS Revitalising Mask

The pre-party mask

Decree SOS Revitalising Mask

One to save for special occasions, Decree's sheet mask truly delivers when it comes to an instant glow. The 100 per cent coconut-fibre mask is soaked in a combination of hyaluronic acid, pore-refining niacinamide, brightening liquorice root and calming rosewater. Apply to dry skin and relax for at-least 15 minutes to ensure maximum results.

What's more, this is perhaps the most plant-conscious sheet mask out there, combining a biodegradable mask with coconut-derived paper packaging.

Tula 24-7 Ultra Hydration Serum

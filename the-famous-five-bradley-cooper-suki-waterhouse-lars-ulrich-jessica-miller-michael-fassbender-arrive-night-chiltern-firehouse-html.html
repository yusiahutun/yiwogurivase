Like the adventurous group of friends made famous by the children's book series, Bradley Cooper, Suki Waterhouse, Lars Ulrich, Jessica Miller and Michael Fassbender turned out in full party force on Saturday night.

The group of friends were spotted making their way into celebrity nightspot Chiltern Firehouse together, all dressed casually for the occasion.

Model Suki, 22, dressed down in a blue striped dress, Each x Other leather jacket and brown beanie hat for the night out, whereas boyfriend Bradley, 39, chose jeans, a jumper and a baseball cap.

Party time: Metallica drummer Lars Ulrich was seen arriving in the back of a taxi with Suki Waterhouse and Bradley Cooper

The couple were joined by Metallica drummer Lars, 50, and his fiancée Jessica Miller, 29, who is also a model.

The Limitless actor even traveled by helicopter to watch friend Lars play with his band Metallica at Glastonbury festival last weekend, with the pair spotted hanging out backstage with Michael Fassbender at the music festival.

Whilst Bradley and Suki have been seen enjoying themselves at the tennis, the Inglorious Bastards actor Michael was spotted at Silverstone to watch Lewis Hamilton attempt to beat his Mercedes rival Nico Rosberg and win the British Grand Prix on Sunday.

Starry night: Bradley and Suki were joined by Michael Fassbender at London nightspot the Chiltern Firehouse

On Saturday Bradley and Suki were seen in the Royal Box to watch the men's singles semi-final match on day 11 of the Wimbledon tennis tournament, giggling and enjoying each others company.

Suki and Bradley have been enjoying a lot of time together since he has finished filming American Sniper with Sienna Miller.

Biker chic: Suki teamed her blue striped dress with a black biker jacket and beanie hat for the night out

But Suki may well be relocating to Los Angeles as a source told The Sun that their nearly 18-month relationship has become very serious.

‘The next step is her moving into his mansion in LA,' a source told British newspaper. ‘Nobody had this relationship down to last.’

‘Both Suki and Bradley’s friends were convinced it would be over within a couple of months because of the 17-year age gap and that they live in different countries. But they are keen to get serious.'

Happy couple: Lars and fiancée model Jessica Miller looked in high spirits as they made their way to the London nightspot

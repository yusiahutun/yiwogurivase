Singer Melissa Etheridge marries Nurse Jackie creator Linda Wallem

May 30, 2024
blog

    Singer Melissa Etheridge has tied the knot with her partner Linda Wallem.The Grammy Award-winning musician and TV producer Linda said 'I do' in a ceremony held at the San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, California on Saturday.

    On the morning of her big day Melissa tweeted a photo of her shoes next to a colourful flower pot with the caption: "A gorgeous, perfect, beautiful day to get married. #shoesonlylove."

    Magazine reports that as well as Melissa's four children, Bailey Jean, 17, Beckett, 15, Johnnie Rose and Miller Steven, both seven, wedding guests included presenter Chelsea Handler, actress Rosie O'Donnell, star Jane Lynch, actor Peter Facinelli and singer Sia Furler.The outdoor service saw hit maker Melissa perform an original song for Linda, which one guest described as 'magical'.

    "It was such a magical moment," a wedding guest told . "They were so happy. It was really beautiful."The invitee added that Melissa wore a grey suit for the occasion while her bride wore a white dress.

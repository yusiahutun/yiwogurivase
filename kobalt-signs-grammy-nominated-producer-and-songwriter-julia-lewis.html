Kobalt Signs Grammy-Nominated Producer And Songwriter JULiA LEWiS

June 01, 2024
blog

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) – Kobalt recently finalized a deal with the highly acclaimed producer and songwriter JULiA LEWiS, renowned for his Latin, pop, and hip-hop contributions. His extensive catalog, boasting over 3 billion Spotify streams, features standout hits like Bad Bunny and Arcángel’s “La Jumpa” and NBA Youngboy’s “Kacey Talk.”

LEWiS, a versatile multi-instrumentalist skilled in piano, guitar, bass, and saxophone, has significantly contributed to collaborations with Anitta, Rauw Alejandro, Shenseea, and Chinese sensation LAY. He was happy with the partnership, saying, “I’m so happy to have formed this partnership with a great team. I couldn’t ask for a better creative team than Kobalt – within our first week of working together, we got a major cut, which turned into a single. I’m excited for what’s to come.”

Jamie Kinelski, Senior Vice President at Kobalt, said, “JULiA is truly unique as his deep musical vocabulary and talent spans pop, hip-hop, and Latin music. Leslie, myself, and the entire creative team at Kobalt couldn’t be more thrilled to partner with him and The Revels Group to help him excel in this next phase of his career.”

LEWiS is managed by Matt Geffen, Jamil Davis, and Matt Bauerschmidt of The Revels Group and represented by Andrew Orcutt of Orcutt Legal. This collaboration signifies a pivotal moment for LEWiS and adds another layer to Kobalt’s already impressive and diverse roster.

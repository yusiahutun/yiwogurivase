Nickelback Announces Las Vegas Concert Series

February 15, 2024
blog

LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) — Canadian rock icons Nickelback announced that they are returning to Las Vegas for a five-night run at The Joint at Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in 2018.

Nickelback’s Las Vegas shows are scheduled for February 23, 24, 27 and March 2 and 3, 2018. Monster Truck, another Canadian rock band, will open for all five shows.

The shows will be Nickelback’s first performances in the market in almost seven years and will constitute their only North American dates for 2018. Nickelback just wrapped a 45-date tour of North America in support of their ninth studio album Feed The Machine this past summer.

Following their Vegas run, Nickelback will embark on the International leg of the Feed The Machine Tour throughout the remainder of 2018, with dates still TBA.

How to register for KCB Mobi loan

KCB has ensured that the KCB Mobi Loan is available through two convenient mobile banking platforms. You can access it by:

  • Using USSD code *522#
  • Downloading the KCB App for Apple, Windows or Android phone

KCB Mobi Loan is available to existing KCB customers or to aspiring customers who don’t have an account yet. The application procedure is different for the two groups though.

If you don’t have a KCB account

  • Go to your M-PESA
  • Go to Lipa na M-PESA and enter Paybill # 522522
  • In the account number section, enter your ID number in the following format. ID[ID number] e.g. ID12345678
  • Enter a starting amount (a small one if you mind. It will be deposited into your new account) and finish the transaction
  • You will receive an SMS notification with a one-time PIN that you can use to access your new account. Remember to change it once you get into your account
  • You will need the PIN to access your account using the USSD code or when configuring your KCB App for the first time

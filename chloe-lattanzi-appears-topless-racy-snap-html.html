Candid: The singer-songwriter revealed last month that she would be writing a book ... hinting it would be about her struggles with drugs and alcohol 

Last month, Chloe revealed she is penning a book for fans, hinting that it will be about her past struggles with anorexia and substance abuse.

Taking to Instagram at the time, Chloe, who is accompanying the book with a documentary and album will be 'from the heart.'

She then asked fans for their input, encouraging them to send her questions about her life that she could answer in the autobiographical book.

'So I could address this in my book. Or if you ever suffered from ed [eating disorders]/mental illness and felt as if we were travelling together [sic],' she wrote in her post.  

In 2012, the singer-songwriter spent seven months in rehab in order to deal with her struggles with cocaine, alcohol and anti-depressant abuse.

In a frank confession, Chloe said by the age of 22 she was spending $US130 a day [$140 AUD] on cocaine and a bottle of vodka.

Speaking to the Mail on Sunday in 2013, Chloe was open about her demons and candid with fans about her struggle with anxiety.

It is accompanied by a 1,700 sq ft guest house, several other smaller buildings and a perfectly circular, 700 sq ft swimming pool. The house is also surrounded by lush lawns and dozens of palm trees.

The oceanfront estate is situated on the sparsely populated southwestern side of Maui, far away from Kahului, the largest city on the island. The house is about 19km (12 miles) south of Kihei, the nearest major city, and is flanked by state parkland and rocky lava fields.

Bezos and Sánchez’s bittersweet romance

But the couple has had its fair share of controversies over the years involving leaked texts, blackmail – and that billion-dollar divorce, of course. In 2019, the Amazon founder accused the owner of The National Enquirer of “extortion and blackmail” after the tabloid newspaper allegedly threatened to publish intimate photos and text messages between Bezos and Sánchez, according to The Guardian.

The tabloid owner’s connection with former US president Donald Trump, the involvement of Lauren’s brother Michael Sánchez, and several other factors also became the talk of the town at around the same time.

However, the celebrity couple appears to have moved past it all. Bezos and Sánchez are often spotted cruising around the world. They made their first public appearance as a couple at a Wimbledon tennis tournament in London, right after the billionaire’s divorce in July 2019, according to The Daily Mail.

In August 2019, Bezos and Sánchez were also seen strolling through St. Tropez and cruising off the coast of Spain, in the Balearic Islands, aboard media mogul David Geffen’s superyacht. The duo has made several public appearances since then, including one in India.

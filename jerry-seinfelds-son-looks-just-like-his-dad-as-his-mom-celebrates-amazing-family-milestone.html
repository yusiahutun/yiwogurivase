Jerry Seinfeld's son looks just like his dad as his mom celebrates amazing family milestone

July 05, 2024
blog

The Seinfeld star’s son looks just like him!

    The apple doesn't fall far from the tree, as proven by Jerry Seinfeld's 20-year-old son, Julian, who recently completed his freshman year at Duke University. 

    With a striking resemblance to his famous father, Julian's milestone was met with heartfelt celebrations from his proud parents.

    Over the weekend, Jerry's wife, Jessica, 50, took to Instagram to share her excitement over the family's latest accomplishment.

     In an Instagram Story, the author of Vegan At Times posted a photo of Julian standing in his dorm room with a green hoodie surrounded by packed bags. 

    Jessica captioned the post: “Sophomore year” with a tick sign tagging her son in the post. 

    Raising two fingers triumphantly, Julian's likeness to his comedian dad is unmistakable. 

    In earlier posts, Julian is seen donning Duke University's colors – a white shirt and blue shorts, with his mom commenting: "Year one done @julianseinfeld!"

    The family's pride in Julian doesn't stop there. To mark his 20th birthday, his parents shared a touching tribute on social media, showcasing rarely-seen photos of their handsome son. 

    The images feature Julian flashing a relaxed smile while posing for the camera, his resemblance to both his parents evident in his charming smile and expressive eyes. 

    Jessica accompanied the photos with a heartfelt message, writing: "Happy 20th Birthday to @julianseinfeld!

    Your work ethic enables you to balance a heavy college course load with a job you love. Your sharply tuned BS detector suffers not one fool. The moral high bar you set for those around you keeps us all in line. You chose your friends wisely, and treat them all like family."

    She continued to praise her son, saying: "You are such a phenomenal and gorgeous human being. Your goodness is greatness, especially in this world today. I love you, my sun." 

    Jerry also chimed in with his own sweet comment, adding: "We love you so much, Jules. Happy Special Birthday this year!!" Julian responded to his mom's post with a few heart emojis.

    Actress Ali Wentworth joined the birthday wishes, exclaiming, "Happy Birthday @julianseinfeld, how are you 20????? I met you as a baby! And now you're a powerhouse! What a human! Let's celebrate." 

    In a 2019 interview with Us Weekly, Jerry discussed his approach to parenting, stating: "We just try to build decent human beings. We're not trying to beat them over the head that they have to give back. … The only way to really do it, we believe, is by doing it yourself and modeling it and hopefully they follow."

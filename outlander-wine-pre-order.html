Pre-Order Your Outlander Wines - Outlander TV Show-Themed Wine Exists

July 24, 2024
blog

Fans of Outlander know vino plays an important role in the series, and now they can indulge in a Jamie Fraser-approved glass in time for their next binge-watching session. Lot18 has created four special Outlander-themed wines in time for the holidays, with each designed specifically for a character in the series (Claire gets two, of course). Choices range from a white wine blend (named for 1940s-era Claire Randall) and a pinot noir (for 1743 Claire Fraser) to cabernet sauvignon (for Frank Randall) and a red blend (for Jamie Fraser).

Caitlyn Jenner Rocks Kate Middleton's Iconic Blue Wrap Dress -- Who Wore It Better?!

March 30, 2024
blog

Caitlyn Jenner is taking style cues from a princess!

The "I Am Cait" star stepped out in Los Angeles this week in a figure-flattering blue Issa London wrap dress. She completed her elegant ensemble with black sling-backs, a matching handbag and simple stud earrings.

But, the 65-year-old fashionista wasn't the only star to wear this fancy frock!

Kate Middleton donned the design to announce her engagement to Prince William back in 2010. The brunette beauty paired the V-neck midi with a dainty necklace, black pumps, and, of course, her matching sapphire ring.

Cait also decided to tie the bow in the front, instead of the back, like the Duchess of Cambridge.

Both ladies looked fantastic in the show-stopping look, but we have to give the nod to Kate. The mother-of-two made the $900 dress fly off the shelves instantly, marking one of many of her most iconic fashion moments.

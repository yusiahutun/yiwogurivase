SZA biography

Solana Imani Rowe was born on 8th November 1989 in St Louis, Missouri, in the United States. Later, her parents relocated to Maplewood, New Jersey, where he was raised. Her father worked as an executive producer for CNN while her mother was an executive at AT&T Inc. She has not offered any information about their names.

The singer was born and raised to a Christian mother and a Muslim. Therefore, SZA's background was orthodox Islam. She was brought out alongside an elder half-brother known as Tiffany Daniels and an older brother known as Daniel. Daniel is a talented rapper commonly known by the stage name Manhattan.

While growing up, she attended a Muslim preparatory school every day after her regular schooling. Later, she joined Columbia High School for her high school education. She was active in sporting activities such as gymnastics and cheerleading in school. She graduated in 2008.

Career beginnings and rise to fame

SZA began her career by recording with her friends and neighbours with many beats from the internet. After that, she self-released her debut EP titled See.SZA.Run on 29th October 2012. On 10th April 2013, she released her second EP, titled S.

She promoted the extended play with the release of the Ice Moon music video. On 14th July 2013, she was signed by the Top Dawg record label. In October 2013, she went on a four-show tour with the Swedish band Little Dragon. The tour began on 17th October at the El Rey Theater in Los Angeles and ended on 24th October at the Music Hall of Williamsburg, Brooklyn, NY.

Who is Olivia Culpo?

She is an American star born on 8 May 1992 in Rhode Island, United States. She is the daughter of Peter Culpo (father) and Susan Curran (mother). Olivia has four siblings and is the middle child. Her siblings are Sophia Culpo, Aurora Culpo, Gus Culpo, and Peter Culpo.

The personality grew up in Edgewood, Cranston. Her parents are multiracial, and she has Italian and Irish ethnicities. She attended St. Mary Academy – Bay View, an all-girls Catholic school in Rhode Island.

Culpo began studying the cello in second grade. She also played in the Rhode Island Philharmonic Youth Orchestra, the Rhode Island Philharmonic Chamber Ensemble, the Bay View Orchestra, and the Rhode Island All-State Orchestra.

Before going to college, Olivia Culpo attended the Brevard Music Center in Brevard, North Carolina, for two summers, and she performed with the Boston Accompanietta.

After high school, she enrolled at Boston University, a private university in Boston, Massachusetts. However, Culpo did not graduate. Instead, she ventured into pageantry.

How To Wear A Beanie

September 30, 2024
blog

Street Styled

Melodie Jeng//Getty Images

A beanie is a functional winter must-have that goes with more looks than you'd think. Play with the streetwear aesthetic of now by adding a fitted beanie to a modern leather trench and sneaker pairing.

This iteration from Parisian brand Ami Alexandre Mattiussi is crafted in beige wool with a playful contrasting heart patch adorning the front.

With Cold Weather Classics

Diego Zuko

You can never go wrong with a neutral wool coat, graphic T-shirt, and light-wash denim. This outfit formula is effortless, probably already exists in your closet, and looks even better when worn with a classic black beanie.

Johnstons of Elgin Ribbed cashmere beanie

Johnstons of Elgin Ribbed cashmere beanie

Johnstons of Elgin always nails cold-weather essentials, so it's no surprise that the brand's take on the classic beanie is a fashion-girl favorite.

Naked Cashmere CHARLIE

This cashmere beanie from Naked Cashmere is both chic and functional, making it an easy addition to your fall and winter rotation.

Add Some Color

Not only is a beanie the perfect way to keep your ears warm, it's also an easy way to incorporate color into an otherwise neutral ensemble. Add a pop of color with a baby pink, orange or powder blue hat.

Acne Studios Pansy N Face Appliquéd Wool Beanie by Acne Studios

Acne Studios Pansy N Face Appliquéd Wool Beanie by Acne Studios

This statement-making hat will leave you feeling nostalgic for fall mornings in high school. Keep it modern and adult-appropriate with tailored separates. 

The Elder Statesman Hot Dye Watchman Cap

The Elder Statesman Hot Dye Watchman Cap

For a pop of bright color, look to this option from LA based designer The Elder Statesman.

Color Me Cozy

Diego Zuko

Mix and match fabrics, colors, and volume for a more dimensional look. To keep things semi-streamlined, opt for a beanie that matches one of the hues in your outfit.

Rag & Bone Addison Fine-Knit Beanie

Rag & Bone Addison Fine-Knit Beanie

This forest green beanie works as a neutral for all your seasonal looks. 

Ganni Wool-Blend Beanie by Ganni

This wool blend beanie by cult favorite brand Ganni will keep you warm on even the coldest of days.

With Monochromatic Black

Head-to-toe black is a city girl styling favorite—everything matches, and the versatility is endless. Topping off an oversized suiting look with a slouchy black beanie is an easy way to remain put together, even in frigid temperatures.

Sofia Cashmere Fox Fur Pompom Beanie

Credit: bergdorfgoodman.com

For added playfulness and warmth, look to Sophia Cashmere's ultra cozy pom-pom option, complete with ribbing for texture.

Cream Of The Crop

Diego Zuko

What's more elegant than all white winter outerwear? Not much. Leave it to Pernille Teisbaek to give us the seasonal inspiration we crave. Style your matching beanie with a belted soft trench.

Gray Matters

Creams, grays, and maroons look sophisticated when styled together. Pair your grey beanie with a chunky cream knit layered over a side-slit sweater dress. Then, add a statement knee-high boot.

Rosie Huntington-Whitely cosies up in this season's must-have coat

June 04, 2024
blog

With the pandemic forcing many of us to spend more time inside, when we do venture out, we want to stay as cosy as possible.

Luckily for us, this season's must-have coat is designed to do just that. Enter the quilted jacket, also aptly known as the duvet coat, guaranteed to keep you warm during those winter months.

Instagram

Ever the style muse, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley demonstrated how the staple is firmly part of her autumn/winter wardrobe.

The quilted style is also popular among the fashion crowd and it's clear it's here to stay; it was all over Instagram feeds in early spring and is regaining popularity in the colder months - whether in bright statement colours or neutral tones.

The versatility and timelessness of the style means you can wear for years to come, so if you're looking to treat yourself to a new coat this year, this one is a worthwhile - and beautifully practical - investment. From designer buys to more affordable versions, shop 10 of our favourite quilted coats below.

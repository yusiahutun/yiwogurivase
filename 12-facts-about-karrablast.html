Karrablast is a Bug-type Pokémon

As its classification suggests, Karrablast belongs to the Bug-type category, which makes it vulnerable to Flying, Rock, and Fire-type attacks. This small, bug-like creature is known for its vibrant blue and black exoskeleton, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Karrablast has a symbiotic relationship with Shelmet

An interesting aspect of Karrablast is its unique evolution process. It can only evolve when it engages in battle with a Shelmet. During the battle, Karrablast will bite into Shelmet’s shell, swapping their exoskeletons. This symbiotic interaction leads to the evolution of both Pokémon, transforming Karrablast to Escavalier and Shelmet to Accelgor.

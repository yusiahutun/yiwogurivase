LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – L.A. Live, AEG’s 4 million square foot downtown sports and entertainment district, will officially bear the Peacock name as of July 11, 2023. In a multi-year naming rights agreement between AEG, a leading sports and live entertainment company and Peacock, NBCUniversal’s premium streaming service, the 7,100-capacity concert and special events venue, formerly known as Microsoft Theater, will be renamed Peacock Theater. In addition, the district’s 40,000-square-foot open-air plaza, formerly known as XBOX Plaza, will be known as Peacock Place.

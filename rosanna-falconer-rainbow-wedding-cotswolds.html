Matthew Williamson's brand consultant has the most AMAZING rainbow-themed wedding

May 29, 2024
blog

This wedding will be going straight on to your Pinterest board

    The wedding of dreams was held in the Cotswolds at the weekend, and we bet you've never seen such a magical set-up. Rosanna Falconer – the Brand Consultant for Matthew Williamson - married her fiancé George Ryan in a colourful, high fashion wedding. With afternoon tea at the beautiful Painswick hotel, guests then progressed to dinner and dancing at Rosanna's family home. The stunning bride wore a fully bespoke dress by Matthew Williamson, and we've never seen such an exquisite looking wedding dress. The breathtaking, floor-length ivory gown featured a mixture of lace and a plethora of rainbow embroidery, which started at her waist and finished at the end of her train. Her adorable little bridesmaids wore dresses in the Matthew Williamson Rosanna Trellis fabric, which is Rosanna's eponymous print. Fashion fans kept up with the day's events on social media thanks to the hashtag #rohemiaryan.

    Rosanna's dress was bespoke Matthew Williamson (@CamillaMorton)

    No detail went untouched; from the whimsical life-size unicorns, a glittering dance floor, an abundance of rainbow flowers and real Shetland ponies carrying confetti. Yes, really! The invites even had an ultra-chic touch - they were custom-made by illustrator Ellie Vandoorne - Rosanna's childhood friend.

    Bridesmaids in Matthew Williamson Rosanna Trellis fabric, which is Rosanna's eponymous print (@annaserrap)

    The star-studded guest list included Matthew Williamson (but of course), actress Olivia Grant, HELLO! Fashion Monthly's very own The London Chatter (who looked gorgeous in Temperley London) Niomi Smart, designer Stuart Vevers and Made in Chelsea star and jewellery designer Rosie Fortescue.

    Unicorns, rainbows and flowers were part of the magical theme (@mcjohnson1986)

    The former Made in Chelsea star took to Instagram to post her wedding outfit of the day - keeping in with the ethereal theme - wearing a maxi dress by Needle & Thread. She captioned the shot: "It's wedding time for @rosannafalconer and I have so far been blown away by such an incredible day."

    Rosie Fortescue wore Needle & Thread's rainbow dress - and Rosanna wore the skirt version the next day (@rosiefortescue/ @rosannafalconer)

    The eye-catching design boasted sequins, pastel rainbow tiers made in the finest tulle and was a totally chic, frothy, iridescent combo. Rosanna wore the skirt version of the show-stopping dress the next day as she posed with her new husband and shared her post wedding ootd on her Instagram stories.

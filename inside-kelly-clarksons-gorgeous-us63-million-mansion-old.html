It may have taken four years, but singer-songwriter and talk show host Kelly Clarkson has sold her gorgeous lake house in Hendersonville, Tennessee for US$6.3 million.

The original listed price was US$8.75 million in 2017, with the most recent reduction earlier this year showing the house on the market for US$7.49 million. Nevertheless, there was still considerable profit to be hand – Clarkson bought the property in 2013 for US$2.8 million.

Not only is the house beautiful, but its setting is to die for. Located on the banks of Old Hickory Lake, 40km (25 miles) up the Cumberland River from Nashville, the water views beyond the 1.6 hectares (four acres) of lawn are exceptional. There is also a long gated driveway to the entrance motor court with centre fountain.

The grounds of the Because of You singer’s home are dotted with mature trees, emerald-green lawns and even a sand volleyball court. It has English-style gardens, a conservatory and a large pool terrace with a pavilion for outdoor dining with a fireplace – all accessed via a double-sided curved staircase descending from the upper terrace. Oh, and there’s a boathouse too.

The neighbourhoods surrounding Old Hickory Lake have drawn many Nashville stars over the years, including Taylor Swift, Johnny and June Carter Cash, Conway Twitty, Ricky Skaggs and Loretta Lynn. It has long been a recreational spot for country music performers; a place where tour schedules and other pressures evaporate with fishing, boating and dining at the nearby marina restaurants.

The 20,121 sq ft house has plenty of space for family, friends and overnight guests. The home’s entrance opens to a grand foyer with soaring double staircases, and the heart of the home is filled with fun touches for casual entertaining, particularly the cowboy bar with its saddle bar stools.

In total the house has seven bedrooms – one of which is a large master suite with a seating area in front of a fireplace and stunning master bath. It boasts 11 bathrooms, a family room, cinema and a children’s room with four built-in bunk beds and a good-sized play area. There’s a billiards/game room, a large gym, a two-level office and lift.

Clarkson has experienced several highs and lows in the past year. She announced in June 2020 that she was divorcing husband Brandon Blackstock after seven years of marriage, which is still ongoing. However, it was also announced that her Emmy-winning talk show, The Kelly Clarkson Show, will be taking over Ellen DeGeneres show’s time slot when it halts production in 2022. Clarkson recently bought a US$5.4 million home in the Toluca Lake neighbourhood of Los Angeles, just 19km (12 miles) from the studios.

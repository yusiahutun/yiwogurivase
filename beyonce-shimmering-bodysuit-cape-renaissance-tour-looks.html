Beyonc's Shimmering Bodysuit &amp; Cape Match Her Holographic Horse

October 04, 2024
blog

If there’s one thing Beyoncé will do, it’s bring the glamour.

The superstar took the stage this week in Philadelphia, amid her Renaissance World Tour, in three dazzling looks. One was a shimmering silver bodysuit with bulbous sleeves, a matching long cape, and thigh-high boots.

In photos shared on Instagram, Bey poses atop her iconic holographic horse (first seen on the Renaissance album cover), which perfectly mirrors her sparkly silver look. She further accessorizes the maximalist ensemble with diamond earrings and silver eye shadow, and wears her hair in long flowy waves.

Another of the “Break My Soul” singer’s dazzling outfits was a sparkly iteration of the little black dress, embellished with cutouts all over and subtle fringe. In the Insta pics, she wears the mini with sheer black opera gloves, black sandals with clear crystals, and layers of diamond jewelry—drop earrings, a tennis bracelet, and a large ring—from Tiffany & Co.

And mimicking another outfit she wore earlier in the tour, Beyoncé also stepped out onstage in Philly in head-to-toe bedazzled camo. The set included a long-sleeved crop top in the classic green print—though embellished with tiny crystals all over—and matching hot shorts that were attached to her heeled boots, like pantaboots or chaps.

Beyoncé has been serving look after dazzling look on her world tour, and earlier this week even gave a nod to her Beyhive of fans with her onstage outfit: a crystal-bedazzled net dress, with the netting featuring a subtle honeycomb design.

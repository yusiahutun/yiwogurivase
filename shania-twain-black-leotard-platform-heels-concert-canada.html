Shania Twain is a total goddess in tiny leotard fresh pictures

The You're Still The One singer pulled out all the stops with another show-stopping stage outfit in Canada

    Shania Twain, 58, has put on another age-defying display while performing her Queen of Me tour at the Scotiabank Arena in Canada, sporting a tiny black leotard and platform heels!

    The singer showcased her seemingly never-ending legs in the daring ensemble which was comprised of a sheer leotard with a train, layered with a camouflage waistcoat. The star embellished the look with multiple gold chains around her neck and spotty fishnet tights.

    The star's makeup was bright and bold for the occasion with a pop of pink lip color and dramatic winged eyeliner.

    Shania's performance was energetic, and she was seen kicking her envy-inducing legs around as she belted out her hits and at one point she climbed onto a motorbike on stage.

Viewers of My Big Fat Fabulous Life recently learned about Angie Burdett, the long-lost sister of Whitney Thore. The show is back for season 11 and now fans will get to meet Angie.

But before that let us help you know where she is from, her age, her job, her husband, and her children. The answer unfolds as this article proceeds.

Meet Angie Burdett, Whitney Thore Sister

Whitney Way Thore disclosed in the eleventh season of My Big Fat Fabulous Life that she has an undiscovered long-lost sister named Angie Burdett. In season 11 of My Big Fat Fabulous Life, Whitney returns to discuss her life path. It was recently discovered that Whitney has a long-lost sister named Angie whom she was unaware of.

The fitness app entrepreneur and reality star rose to fame after disclosing her weight loss techniques on YouTube. Whitney created dancing videos of herself, which caught people’s interest and eventually led to the possibility of her own reality program.

Whitney’s relationship with her family is shown in the episode of My Big Fat Fabulous Life. She always got along well with both of her parents, Barbara “Babs” and Glenn Thore. Hunter Thore, Whitney’s older brother, is four years Whitney’s senior. The Thore family experienced tragedy in 2022 when her mother Babs tragically passed away at the age of 76 following a stroke.

Whitney, Glenn, and Hunter have been mourning her loss and miss her terribly ever since her passing.

Whitney learned about her half-sister Angie, about whom not much is known when her mother suffered her second stroke. After his wife died, Whitney gave Glenn something to do, so he assisted her in moving into her new home. They engaged in joint activities like swimming laps.

However, Glenn’s revelation that she shared a child with Jackie, a woman he dated before meeting Babs, was unexpected.

Babs experienced her first stroke in 2017. According to Whitney, her mother was identified with cerebral amyloid angioplasty, an “untreatable progressive condition caused by proteins in the blood vessels of the brain that can cause dementia, seizures, or like in my mom’s case, brain bleeding (hemorrhagic strokes),” as HollywoodLife reported.

Babs suffered another stroke in December 2021, despite the doctors’ predictions to the Thore family that she would have more strokes in the future. Babs, the mother of Whitney, suffered her final stroke in November 2022, which finally caused her death, from a condition called cerebral amyloid angiopathy.

Glenn previously knew about Angie, but he didn’t actually meet her until after Babs had passed away. Hunter, Whitney’s brother, advised that Glenn create a bucket list to help him deal with Babs’ passing on My Big Fat Fabulous Life. Glenn has a bucket list that includes visiting Switzerland and getting a tattoo.

Viewers will witness Glenn call Angie later in the season and arrange a meeting with her, along with Whitney and Hunter. It will be touching to see the family members reunite in My Big Fat Fabulous Life season 11 when they first meet.

Angie Burdett Age

Angie Burdett turned 55 in 2023. She was born in 1968.

Angie Burdett Job

Angie Burdett reportedly worked as a Bartender at Oysters Rockefeller. Her career employment status is unclear.

Is Angie Burdett Married?

Yes, Angie Burdett is currently married. Her husband is named Shanon Burdett. On Angie’s birthday in 2018, Shanon wished his wife, “Happy birthday to My ANGIE. I love you have a great day baby.”

In September 2022, Angie wrote, “A big shoutout to my husband for being so awesome! My dad has been in the hospital for 15 days now we are at Rehab for at least another 2 weeks and he has done anything and everything to help I couldn’t do this without you Love You (My Angie).”

Likewise, on Father’s Day June 2023, Angela again wished her husband, “Happy Father’s Day to my Husband! We are very blessed to have you PawPaw. LoveYou.”

Talking about children, Angie has three; two sons named Cody and Ryne Vincent, and a daughter named Jaime.

Moreover, on her son Cody Vincent’s birthday, she wished, “Happy birthday to my son Cody who is at Navy boot camp at the Great Lakes so we will be celebrating it when we see you at graduation! I could not be more proud of you this is just the beginning of great things to come! I LOVE YOU AND MISS YOU VERY MUCH!!!!!!.”

Also, Cody works at the U.S. Navy. He went to Theodore High School and the University of South Alabama. He is currently single.

Angie had Cody with her one Jimmy Daughdrill. Jimmy also has a daughter named Jaime Daughdrill who Shanon appears to be close to.

On Jamie’s 23rd birthday in February 2021, Angela wished her daughter, “Happy birthday to my amazing daughter that God blessed me with 23 years ago. I couldn’t be more proud of the young lady you are. LOVE YOU THE MOST and hope you have the best day ever. Maddie said to tell her Aunt JJ happy birthday and that shes her favorite aunt ever and she loves you the most.”

In September 2023, Angela shared that Jaime is getting married to her fiance Heath Horton.

Angie’s other son is named Ryne Vincent. Ryne went to Theodore High School.

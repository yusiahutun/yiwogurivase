Online casinos invite the most active players to become loyal and VIP client at an online casino. What does it mean? How does this status influence your career at an online casino? Below, let’s find out all details about this issue.

A loyalty program is an indispensable element of gambling on any website. It is a multi-layer program with additional privileges for the most active players. Thus, being a loyal or VIP client at an online casino enjoying more favorable conditions and getting exclusive perks for gambling.

What Perks to get for VIP client at an online casino?

Although each online casino set unique terms and perks, most of them are similar. So, players are likely to get the next perks:

  • Extra bonuses (cash or free spins) for entering a new level.
  • Cashback, the size of which rises with each level and can reach 25%-25%.
  • More favorable wagering terms.
  • Higher withdrawal limits are set individually for each player.
  • Services of a personal host and manager.
  • Privilege in support and cashout request processing.
  • Access to closed parties and private tournaments.
  • More profitable exchange rates for comp points.

The list of perks becomes larger and more fruitful as gamblers level up to higher levels.

Loyal VS VIP client at an online casino: What is the Difference?

Most casinos offer a loyalty or VIP program, which come as synonyms. In most cases, VIP client at an online casino are participants of higher tiers of the loyalty program. Thus, VIP players are the most successful loyal clients. Yet, in some cases, gambling websites can develop two different programs, namely, the loyalty program and a VIP club. In such cases, the most successful players receive a personal invitation to join the VIP club from the casino administration.

How to Level up in the Loyalty Program at an Online Casino?

Note that each online casino sets its own terms for entering the loyalty program. The most common options include:

  • automatically upon registration;
  • upon making the first deposit;
  • upon placing the first real money bet;
  • upon collecting the required number of comp points.

To level up gamblers should also collect comp points. Comp points are given for making real money bets of certain worth, For example, 1 CP is given for each $5 bet. It means that if you make a $4 bet, you won’t receive a comp point. When the required amount of points is accumulated, players level up to the next tier.

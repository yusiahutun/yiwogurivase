Who is Mike Jackson?

Mike Jackson is a multi-talented individual who has made a name for himself as both a DJ and a builder. Though his exact birth year remains a mystery, Mike was born on November 11 in Hempstead, New York. He was raised by his parents alongside his accomplished brother Jamel D Jackson, who has found success as an author and entrepreneur.

Mike's professional journey aligned perfectly with his wife Egypt Sherrod, a renowned real estate broker as they joined forces in the captivating HGTV series 'Married to Real Estate.' Together, they embarked on the exciting mission of transforming ordinary houses into stunning homes through their smart renovations. Balancing the demands of their busy professional and personal lives, Egypt and Mike showcased their dynamic partnership and turned ordinary spaces into extraordinary ones. Their collaboration didn't stop there. Fearlessly embracing new challenges, Egypt and Mike eagerly competed in the highly anticipated third season of HGTV's beloved home renovation competition series titled 'Rock the Block'.

With their combined expertise, unwavering dedication, and a desire to leave a lasting impact, they aimed to showcase their remarkable skills and create awe-inspiring spaces that captured the imagination. On September 11, 2010, Mike and Egypt tied the knot. They have two beautiful daughters -- Kendall, born in January 2012, and Harper, born in February 2019. Additionally, Mike has a daughter from a previous relationship, further enriching their loving family dynamic. Mike exemplifies a true force of creativity, merging his passions for music and woodworking to craft remarkable experiences and build extraordinary spaces that captivate and inspire.

Mike, also known as DJ Fadel, has excelled in various fields including contracting, building, modeling, acting, and entrepreneurship. DJ Fadelf honed his skills with a single turntable, blending the limited records he had with music videos on TV. Once he got a taste of the DJ life, there was no looking back. His career took him to incredible destinations such as the Bahamas, Japan, Paris, Germany, Jamaica, Spain, Kansas, Berlin, Belgium, Switzerland, Brussels, Las Vegas, L.A, Miami, Kuwait, Chicago, Canada, Atlanta, Detroit, and his hometown of New York, where he left audiences in awe.

Beyond his DJ prowess, Mike co-founded the Jackson Draper Renovations, showcasing his talent as a builder and contractor. He also held the title of co-owner at Ovation Cologne and appeared as a cast member on HGTV shows like 'Married to Real Estate' and 'Rock the Block,' alongside his wife Egypt. Together, they form a dynamic duo that is ready to leave its mark in the world of real estate and home renovation.

At 48, popular TikToker Joyce Seepane has been a side chick to a public figure for 23 years.

Joyce met love when she was 19 years old

In an interview on the Own Narrative Podcast, Joyce said she met her lover when she was going to school, and he stopped his car to talk to her.

"I was still a student in Form Three and he was of marriageable age. He offered to take me home, and I was so taken by the old man. From that day, he never left me alone," she said.

" I was 19 years old, and my man was 30; he was a public figure in the African National Congress (ANC) and I soon became his right-hand person who went everywhere with him," she continued.

The two dated long, but her family disapproved of the relationship because of his age. On the other hand, Joyce had difficulty conceiving, which got her in trouble with her lover's family.

Joyce lover marries another woman

Eventually, the man married another man and had a child with her. Shortly afterwards, Joyce fell pregnant, and the man accepted the pregnancy.

"He did ask to marry me as a second wife after having a child, but remember he is a public figure and it would have been a waste of time; girls used to throw themselves at him because he was famous," she said.

"When I saw that his life was very busy I decided no I couldn't marry him; once I married him I would have to stay at home as he went for political meetings with somebody else," she explained.

Joyce child living with lover's wife

Joyce and her lover are still together to this day, and their son is now 18 years old. Her lover has since built her a house, and he lives with their son at his family's home.

" I live in Johannesburg and my son lives with them; his wife is very soft and understanding," she said.

Although Joyce was reluctant to marry her lover many years ago, she is now willing to marry him.

"Now I am ready to marry him because he is a pensioner, and he is always at home. When you find out the man has other women, let him enjoy," she said.

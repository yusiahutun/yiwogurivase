Who is John Paul Tremblay?

John Paul Tremblay is an actor, comedian, and screenwriter born on 17 May 1968 in Halifax Regional Municipality, Canada. He was raised in the city, where he went to school and met Robb Wells, his future co-star.

His career in the film industry commenced in 1995 when he was featured in the short film The Cart Boy. He is best recognised as Julian from the Trailer Park Boys franchise, and he has appeared in other movies and TV series, including Dear Julian, Rush: Cinema Strangiato 2019, Sunnyvale Shoals, and Randy's Reach.

He is also a podcaster and co-hosts the podcast Trailer Park Boys Presents: Park After Dark alongside Ricky and Bubbles. The trio also teams up on SwearNet, a streaming comedy network. John Paul Tremblay and his friend, actor and comedian Robb Wells, owned J.R. Capone’s, a chain of pizza restaurants on Prince Edward Island.

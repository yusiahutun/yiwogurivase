Dane Cook Has A Fresh New Flavor

April 30, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Comedian and actor Dane Cook announced ‘Fresh New Flavor’ a 36-date comedy tour that will hit major markets in North American, including New York, Atlanta, Chicago and Toronto.

Produced by Live Nation, the tour kicks off at Pearl Concert Theater at Palms Casino Resort in Las Vegas on August 30th and wraps on December 14th at the Paramount Theatre, in Denver.

The news of the tour follows the recent announcement that Cook has partnered with Super Channel to produce a documentary on his life titled, “Brace for Impact: The Dane Cook Story.”

Arket

A black slip skirt in high-quality satin is a great option for pretty much any wardrobe – and this fluted style from Arket is super flattering. It's weighty enough to hang properly, and features a neatly concealed zip (rather than the usual high-street elasticated waistband). Also note the side split, which ensures fluid movement.

Faithfull The Brand

Glossy shades of chocolate and coffee are key this season, and a satiny slip skirt is the most elegant way to work the look. We love the slightly longer-than-average length of Faithfull's piece, which looks especially chic when teamed with the matching shirt.

Ghost

Simple, elegant satin pieces are a Ghost speciality – and the Emma skirt shows just why. Available in black, cream and navy, it features a glossy, liquid-like finish and is cut generously enough to ensure beautiful movement as you walk. Team it with a pair of the new season's key kitten-heel pumps.

Farm Rio

Beloved in its native Brazil, Farm Rio's vivid prints are about to be big news in the UK too. Undeniably joy-inducing, this swirling fucshia and red slip skirt will transform the simplest of separates into a statement outfit. Wear it now with knee-high boots, and block-heel sandals come warmer climes.

Veronica Beard

Veronica Beard

The perfect evening option comes courtesy of Veronica Beard this season. The designer's stretch-silk skirt is cut to fall softly to the floor, making for an especially elegant silhouette. It simply slips on, meaning there's no fussy fastenings to spoil the clean lines.

Rixo

You can always rely on Rixo for a great print, and this season we're truly spoilt for choice. The brand's signature Kelly slip skirt comes in retro florals, classic leopard print and a host of jacquard designs. Wear it on holiday with the matching scarf-style Menno top.

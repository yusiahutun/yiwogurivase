Taylor Swift's 'first date' rule she didn't break for Travis Kelce

September 10, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift is officially ready to talk all things Travis Kelce. The singer didn’t shy from the topic in a recent interview, including touching upon her first date rule that she did not break for the NFL star.

This was Taylor Swift‘s year and Time Magazine agrees after naming the singer the Person Of The Year. Along with a historical breakthrough in her career, the music star’s personal life has gone through major ups and down this year. She broke up with British movie star Joe Alwyn, but then found love again with NFL heartthrob Travis Kelce.

Taylor Swift claims Travis Kelce put her on blast

Taylor Swift is ‘lovestruck’ with Travis Kelce, and it all began when he ‘adorably’ put her on blast after being ‘rejected’ when he attended the Eras tour. Unlike many fans who assumed the celebrity couple‘s first date was when she appeared for the athlete’s match at Arrowhead stadium in September, she shares that their first date was very much private.

Speaking of her first date with the 34-year-old Catching Kelce star, Swift confessed: “This all started when Travis very adorably put me on blast on his podcast, which I thought was metal as hell.”

She added: “We started hanging out right after that. So we actually had a significant amount of time that no one knew, which I’m grateful for, because we got to get to know each other. By the time I went to that first game, we were a couple.

“I think some people think that they saw our first date at that game? We would never be psychotic enough to hard launch a first date.”

As much of Taylor’s love life is in the public eye – she has a long list of ex lovers – avoiding having the early part of the relationship in public view is how she likes to deal with things. In most cases, whether that is Harry Styles or Tom Hiddleston, the singer isn’t often snapped with a beau until they’re far into dating.

Taylor Swift shares thoughts on having a ‘public relationship’

It is no secret Taylor’s relationship with Joe Alwyn was very private. The couple were hardly ever spotted publicly and even though the celebrity thanked Joe in her Grammy speech, the actor kept it under the wraps for the most part.

After Joe, it looks like the Cornelia Street crooner is ready to welcome a new outlook of love. The music icon shares what it means to her to date someone else in the public eye.

The 33-year-old explained: “When you say a relationship is public, that means I’m going to see him do what he loves, we’re showing up for each other, other people are there and we don’t care.”

Almost as if to draw a closure for fans, the songstress admitted what it means to her to keep the relationship private and away from the limelight.

“The opposite of that is you have to go to an extreme amount of effort to make sure no one knows that you’re seeing someone. And we’re just proud of each other,” she tells Time.

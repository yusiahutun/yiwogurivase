Adam Sandler Kids, Daughters in You Are So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah

August 09, 2024
blog

Sadie Sandler

Sadie Sandler is Adam Sandler and Jackie Sandler’s first child born on May 6, 2006.

Sandler told Access Hollywood about his epiphany as a father when his firstborn was born. “When my kid was born, I was so nervous. I didn’t know what I felt. Five minutes later, maybe 10 minutes later, it was me and the kid and a nurse. We walked down alone to just check the vital signs and all that stuff, and I had a chemical reaction in my body, where I loved the kid so much, and I was so nervous for her, and that’s when I lost my mind for the kid.” 

“So, it took 10 minutes to become a good dad,” he added. “For the first 10 minutes, I was just lost.”

Sadie went on to go on films with her father like You Don’t Mess with the Zohan and was the first to break the news that Adam will be a daddy to two. He recalled on Live with Regis and Kelly, “I came home at night and [Jackie] kept saying ‘Sadie wants to give you a present,’ and normally Sadie falls asleep around 8. It was like quarter of 9 and I just got back from working.

He continued, “And then Sadie hands me a little something, like a gift. And I was ‘Aww, that’s nice Sadie, thank you.’ And then Jackie kept saying ‘Open it up, open it up.’ And I open it up and it was one of those pregnancy [tests] and I was like ‘Woowww.'”

 Adam also talked about his daughter’s crushes on the Ellen DeGeneres Show who was 12-years-old at the time. “You know, I drive around with [her and] her friends, and I hear them talking about boys now,” he said. “It’s amazing, the feelings I had about the same boys five years ago. When they would mention the kid’s name, I’d be like, ‘I love that kid.’ Now, I get very jumpy.” Adam admitted that he now feared boys with “little tiny mustaches” and bodies that “aren’t making much sense.”

At Sadie’s Bat Mitzvah, Adam invited Adam Levine from Maroon 5 to play some songs for his daughter. “Oh, she hugged me so much, my Sadie,” he told Jimmy Kimmel about the outcome of the performance.

Jennifer Aniston was invited to the ceremony, but couldn’t attend, “She had stuff going on, so she sent a gift. “One of the best gifts Sadie received out of everybody,” Adam recounted.

“What was it?” Kimmel asked.

“$1.8 million dollars. We couldn’t believe it, and Sadie blew it,” he laughed.

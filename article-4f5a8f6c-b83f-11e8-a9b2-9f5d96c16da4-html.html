Local sculptor to speak at LRMA | News

March 13, 2024
blog

Local sculptor Jason Kimes will be the speaker at noon Thursday when Lauren Rogers Museum of Art presents ArtTalk.

Kimes will discuss the design and fabrication process of his sculptures. He received his bachelor of arts degree in sculpture from The University of Southern Mississippi. His large-scale works have been installed and exhibited across the country, including Eleven, the Deepwater Horizon Memorial. One of his works was also on display in downtown Laurel, off Central Avenue, earlier this year.

We don’t know about you but on November 1, all Halloween decorations come down and the Christmas music comes on. The holidays are officially in full swing. And now, that goes for our shower too. Dove rolled out its first-ever, limited-edition Holiday Treats Collection of all-new Body Wash, Body Scrub, Beauty Bar, and Hand Wash options in delicious holiday scents. Oh, and did I mention it’s all less than $7 at Walmart?

The three unique scents to choose from are peppermint bark inspired by fresh peppermint candy blended with rich, creamy chocolate; cinnamon pumpkin pie, inspired by sweet cinnamon spice, mixed with buttery pumpkin notes; and sugar cookie, inspired by the vanilla nuttiness of freshly baked sugar cookies. Yum.

It’s all about the coziness of the colder months. These products will help you scrub away dead, dry skin, wash with nourishing, gentle cleansers, and hydrate with plant-based moisturizers. Plus, the scent of your shower will transport you into the holiday season even if it’s still a bit far away.

In addition to the Walmart collection, Dove also launched its first-ever mini Body Scrub exclusively at Target for a limited time. It’s available in a Hot Cocoa scent featuring a blend of cocoa and sweet marshmallow, with a touch of cinnamon.

Shop all the yummy new options, below. Hurry – they’re selling out fast!

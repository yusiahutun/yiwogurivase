Dua Lipa's Glittery One-Shoulder Dress Will Be on My 2024 Vision Board

April 04, 2024
blog

Attaining the level of cool that’s omnipresent in Dua Lipa’s wardrobe is now at the top of my New Year’s resolution list.

If you needed any further proof of the “Houdini” singer’s remarkable style, let her latest Instagram photo dump serve as a reminder. In the midst of this holiday season, Lipa has been busy traveling through India, where she delivered back-to-back fashion statements.

In one picture, Lipa posed in a glittery one-shoulder dress from Vivienne Westwood’s Spring 2024 collection. The sparkly piece features a retro maroon, black, and yellow oval pattern set in diagonal stripes, along with red-and-gold striped ribbing details at the neckline. She paired the dress with bulbous gold earrings and matching gold rings.

Instagram

In another snap, the Grammy winner looked effortlessly cool while leisurely posing in the back seat of a convertible. She wore a cropped collarless leather jacket awash in a bright cherry red, a standout piece from Chanel’s 2023 resort collection. She finished off the ensemble with a plain base consisting of a white top, distressed straight-leg jeans, and a black belt. Her luxe accessories included gold dangle drop earrings, black oval sunglasses from Prada, a gold Cartier watch, a gold diamond-embellished bangle bracelet from Tiffany & Co., and a black Hermès Birkin bag.

Instagram

“Happy Holidays from me to youuuuu❤️,” the singer wrote in the caption. “Sending love light health and happiness for the year ahead x.”

Lipa previously explained her bombshell sense of style in a 2017 interview with Harper’s Bazaar.

“I like having one piece that makes a whole outfit and then you can wear everything else that’s quite simple,” she said at the time. “I’ve always loved dressing up. I like it when things look like you’ve just thrown them on and sometimes the things you really do just throw on look the best. I’ve always been inspired by the ’90s, and I like wearing baggy trousers, and blazers with slipdresses and thigh-high boots or leather jackets, and just a mixture of all those together.”

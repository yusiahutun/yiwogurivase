See Jennifer Lopez's Low-Key Girls' Night Out Look

September 18, 2024
blog

When it comes to high-glam style, Jennifer Lopez is in a category all her own. We're talking about a woman who carries a $100,000 Birkin as a gym bag, goes house-hunting in high heels, and, no matter the occasion, is always sporting several carats of diamonds. 

But on Saturday, the singer-slash-actress swapped her trademark aesthetic for a girls' night out look that was uncharacteristically low-key — at least, by J.Lo's standards. In a slideshow of selfies shared to Instagram, aptly captioned "girls' night," Lopez wore an outfit that just about anyone could recreate. For the occasion, she paired a cropped cream-colored turtleneck sweater that featured ribbed detailing with baggy, high-waisted denim with frayed hems and huge back pockets. 

While her outfit was simple and straightforward, J.Lo jazzed things up with her accessories, which included a beige croc-embossed handbag, brown-tinted aviator sunglasses, and oversized gold hoops. Oh, and lest we forget her gargantuan platform heels.  

Beauty-wise, she matched her glossy cherry lips to her freshly-painted red nails and slicked her caramel hair back into a super-sleek bun. 

J.Lo's date for the evening was film producer, Elaine Goldsmith-Thomas, who worked on several of Lopez's movies — including, Maid in Manhattan, Second Act, and her Netflix documentary, Halftime. Together, they are gearing up for the release of another collaboration, the sci-fi action flick Atlas, which premieres on Netflix on May 24. 

In the film, Lopez plays a woman named Atlas Shepherd, a "brilliant but misanthropic data analyst with a deep distrust of artificial intelligence" who "joins a missions to capture a renegade robot with whom she shares a mysterious past." Along with Lopez, the film also stars Simu Liu, Sterling K. Brown, Gregory James Cohen, and Abraham Popoola.

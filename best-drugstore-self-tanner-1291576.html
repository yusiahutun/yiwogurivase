You can achieve a sun-kissed glow without the harmful effects of UV radiation and burning holes in your pocket with the best drugstore self-tanners. As you may be aware, self-tanners are designed to darken the skin, mimicking the effects of sun exposure, through the use of active ingredients like dihydroxyacetone (DHA). 

As per our expertise, a good self-tanner should provide an even, natural-looking tan without streaks or an orange hue. However, differentiating between quality self-tanners and ineffective ones can be a challenging task, especially when presented with a plethora of options. Safe to say, picking the best often comes down to formulation, application method, and user reviews. 

We recommend looking for products with moisturizing ingredients like glycerin or hyaluronic acid to prevent dryness and ensure an even application. Additionally, opt for self-tanners that offer a buildable tan to customize the depth of color. Common ingredients found in self-tanners include DHA, erythrulose, and various botanical extracts. 

With the right product and application technique, drugstore self-tanners can provide a safe and healthy glow year-round. Here we have rounded up the top self-tanners available in drugstores. So read on and take your pick.

    Buying Guide to Pick the Best Drugstore Self-tanner

    When selecting a drugstore self-tanner, consider the following factors to ensure optimal results:

    Ingredients: Look for products containing DHA as the active tanning ingredient. Additionally, seek formulations with moisturizing agents like glycerin or hyaluronic acid to keep your skin hydrated during and after application.

    Formulation: Choose between lotions, mousses, sprays, or the best drugstore tanning oils, based on your preference and skin type. Opt for tinted formulas or guide colors to aid in even application and reduce the risk of streaking.

    Skin Type And Desired Tan Intensity: Consider your skin type (dry, oily, sensitive) and the level of tan intensity you desire. Some products offer gradual tanning effects, allowing you to control the depth of color over time.

    Price And Value: Evaluate the product's price relative to its size, ingredients, and performance. While affordability is a key factor, prioritize value by considering the quality and quantity offered.

    By carefully considering these factors, you can confidently select a drugstore self-tanner that suits your preferences and delivers a natural-looking tan.

    How to Apply Drugstore Self-tanners?

    Achieving a flawless tan with a drugstore self-tanner requires proper application technique. Here are the steps with some additional tips: 

    • Begin by exfoliating your skin to remove dead cells and ensure an even tan. 
    • Moisturize dry areas like elbows, knees, and ankles to prevent excessive absorption of the tanner, unless otherwise specified in a brand’s product usage instructions. 
    • Apply the product in circular motions using a tanning mitt or gloves to avoid streaks. Remember to start with a conservative amount and gradually build up the tan to your desired intensity. 
    • Pay attention to tricky areas such as hands, feet, and face, using sparing amounts and blending well. 
    • Allow ample time for the product to dry before dressing to prevent smudging or transferring.

    FAQ's

    What are the benefits of choosing a drugstore self-tanner?

    Drugstore self-tanners offer affordability and convenience, providing a sun-kissed glow without the harmful effects of UV radiation.

    Are drugstore self-tanners suitable for sensitive skin?

    Many drugstore self-tanners are formulated with gentle ingredients, making them safe for sensitive skin. However, it's recommended to check the product's label for any potential irritants.

    Can drugstore self-tanners deliver a natural-looking tan?

    Yes, drugstore self-tanners can provide a natural-looking tan when applied properly. Opt for products with buildable formulas to customize the intensity of the tan to your liking.

    How long does the tan from a drugstore self-tanner last?

    The longevity of a drugstore self-tanner's tan varies depending on factors such as formulation and application. Typically, the tan can last anywhere from a few days to a week before gradually fading.

    Can I use a drugstore self-tanner on both my face and body?

    Yes, many drugstore self-tanners are suitable for use on both the face and body. However, it's essential to choose products specifically formulated for facial use to avoid potential irritation.

    How can I prevent streaks and uneven color when applying self-tanner?

    To avoid streaks and uneven color, exfoliate and moisturize your skin beforehand. Apply the self-tanner in circular motions using a mitt or glove for even distribution. Allow ample time for the product to dry before dressing.

    Will the self-tanner leave my skin with an orange tint?

    Quality drugstore self-tanners are formulated to provide a natural-looking tan without an orange tint. Look for products with natural ingredients and read reviews to ensure the best results.

    Can I use a drugstore self-tanner on fair or deep skin tones?

    Yes, drugstore self-tanners are available in various shades to suit all skin tones, from fair to deep. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone for the most natural-looking results.

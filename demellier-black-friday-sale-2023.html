DeMellier Black Friday Sale 2023

February 02, 2024
blog

Founded by Mireia Llusia-Lindh, DeMellier has ascended as a go-to handbag label for women in and out of the fashion industry. The brand’s DNA is based on Lindh’s essentials for a well-lived life: suitable companionship, immersive travel, and admirable, yet functional, design. Because of this ethos, perhaps, DeMellier has become a brand even Catherine, Princess of Wales wears on repeat.

For seasoned fanatics and new obsessors alike, the best time of the year to discover Kate’s DeMellier favorites (and ours)—from the leather top-handle mini bag she owns in two colors, to the cult-favorite New York bag—is finally upon us. The brand is ringing in Black Friday by holding its only sitewide sale of the year. DeMellier’s annual Black Friday/Cyber Monday sale opens to the public on November 20, but Bazaar readers can shop the sale early starting today, November 17, with code HARPERSBAZAAR20.

The Princess of Wales has worn the Nano Montreal bag several times over the years. In Scarborough, England, alongside her husband Prince William, she paired it with coordinating head-to-toe neutrals. On a visit to Cardiff, Wales, for Queen Elizabeth II’s Platinum Jubilee celebrations with Prince William and their two elder children, she carried a black version with a poppy-red coat.

The Nano Montreal is a petite, structured crossbody bag that features a removable and adjustable strap, as well as a tubular top handle. It’s a bite-size statement piece, with proportions to fit most smartphones and crucial daily necessities. Think: card case, headphones, lipstick, and keys.

Just like Kate, we may have to own it in more than one color. And with this sale, that’s absolutely possible.

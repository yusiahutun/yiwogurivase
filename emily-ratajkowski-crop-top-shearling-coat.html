Emily Ratajkowski Pairs a Tiny Crop Top With a Shearling Coat

April 21, 2024
blog

Emily Ratajkowski just found the perfect way to incorporate summer clothes into a fall wardrobe.

While walking her dog, Colombo, in the streets of New York City earlier this week, the My Body author was spotted turning the sidewalks into a runway with a striking ensemble. The centerpiece of her look was Mirror Palais’s sold-out black turtleneck crop top, with an underwire hemline sculpted to fit just below the bust.

She paired the head-turning piece with a pair of khaki-green straight-leg pants, cinched at the waist by a black belt from Khaite, and a long caramel coat featuring creamy shearling lining. To cap off the outfit, Ratajkowski added black-framed glasses with yellow-tinted lenses, gold hoop earrings, and black sneakers.

Splash News

This season, the model has clearly put her affinity for statement coats on display. For instance, earlier this month, she was spotted out and about in New York wearing a long gray teddy coat from Mango. To complement the reversible outerwear, she also wore black Danzy jogger sweats, a lime green pair of Loewe’s Cloudtilt sneakers, black Prada shades, and gold hoop earrings.

Prior to that, Ratajkowski stepped out dressed in a sleek leather trench coat in a burnt orange. She tied off the belted waist over the rest of her outfit, which included a black top, a silky black midi skirt from Silk Laundry, dark brown pointed-toe heeled boots, and some large chunky gold hoop earrings.

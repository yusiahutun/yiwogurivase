Meaning of Benz Friendz (Whatchutola) by Future

July 09, 2024
blog

The Future song titled 'Benz Friendz (Whatchutola)' delves into the complexities and superficiality of material possessions and surface-level relationships. The overall theme of the song revolves around the idea that cars, money, and flashy lifestyles are ultimately meaningless when it comes to true human connection and genuine relationships. Future, along with the feature from André 3000, explores various perspectives and cleverly highlights the hollowness of focusing solely on material wealth.One standout lyric in the song is "These cars don't mean shit, these hoes don't mean shit, these clothes don't mean shit, these shows don't mean shit." This lyric reflects the core message of the song, emphasizing that material possessions and outward displays of wealth hold no lasting value or meaning. It challenges the notion that cars, women, clothes, and fame define success or happiness.Another noteworthy lyric is "But if I'm in a wheelchair, you still there?" This line questions the loyalty and commitment of a partner who is only interested in being with someone for their wealth and status. It highlights the importance of having someone who genuinely cares about the individual, regardless of their financial or physical circumstances.In a thought-provoking lyric, André 3000 says, "If yellow seems to be the color in fashion, what happens to all of this good Black pussy he keep ignoring?" This line brings up the issue of racial fetishization and the objectification of women based on their race. It challenges the concept of desirability being solely based on societal trends and explores the importance of acknowledging and valuing every individual as a whole, rather than reducing them to stereotypes.The lyrics "I guess that's why Lois can't be with Clark Kent" and "The girl is only with him because he's touring" highlight the struggles faced by celebrities in finding genuine connections. These lines touch on the difficulty of finding sincerity and authenticity in relationships when one's fame and wealth become a dominant factor in attracting others. It emphasizes the need for individuals to be cautious of those who are solely interested in their celebrity status or perceived monetary value.Furthermore, the mention of a 1994 Toyota Land Cruiser and Future's decision to keep it despite having access to more luxurious vehicles brings to light the idea of sentimentality and the value of simplicity. This lyric suggests that sometimes the most meaningful possessions are the ones that hold sentimental value rather than expensive status symbols. It also challenges the notion that one must constantly upgrade to keep up with the demands of society.In summary, 'Benz Friendz (Whatchutola)' uses clever wordplay and thought-provoking lyrics to explore the shallowness of material possessions and surface-level relationships. The song encourages listeners to prioritize true connections and emotional depth over material wealth. It challenges societal norms and explores themes of loyalty, racial fetishization, authenticity, and the value of sentimental possessions. Ultimately, the song serves as a reminder that true happiness and fulfillment come from genuine human connections, not materialistic pursuits.

Here Are the Lyrics to Kenny Chesney's 'Tip of My Tongue'

May 23, 2024
blog

The country superstar co-wrote the song with pop superstar Ed Sheeran, who helped him bring to life an idea that he'd had, but didn't know how to finish.

"It is an authentic story in my life and idea in my life, about connection," Chesney says. "Once we got into the idea of the song and started exploring, the song, in a lot of ways, wrote itself."

Ross Copperman also joined Chesney and Sheeran in writing the "Tip of My Tongue" lyrics, which are paired with a very progressive track that finds Chesney exploring more new musical ground. The song is set to appear on Chesney's upcoming 18th studio album, which will be his second under a new deal he struck with Warner Music Nashville in 2018 after departing Sony, his label home of more than 20 years.

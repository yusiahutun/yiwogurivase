NeNe Wears Exterminator Costume, Porsha Calls Out Kim's 'Questionable' Character

July 04, 2024

It all went down on Sunday's "Real Housewives of Atlanta" season finale at Eva Marcille's Halloween party.

Eva and Shereé Whitfield coincidentally both came dressed as Cleopatra, Kandi Burruss came as sexy Pennywise from "It," Kenya Moore dressed as a Victoria's Secret angel, Porsha Williams was a zombie bride, Cynthia Bailey turned into a very convincing 50 Cent (better known as 50 Cynt), Kim Zolciak and husband Kroy Biermann dressed as a Playboy Bunny and Hugh Hefner (R.I.P.), and NeNe Leakes and husband Gregg Leakes showed up in an exterminator and roach costume, respectively.

"Y'all did that!" Porsha said of NeNe and Gregg's RoachGate getups. "Y'all are a petty mess. Petty goals! That is couple petty goals right there."

NeNe explained that she decided to be an exterminator "because I just want Kim and her little silly-ass dumb f-cking family to know that we are laughing at your ass, bitch. Hee hee ha ha, bitch, the joke is on you."

But the real drama came after Porsha's best friend, Shamea Morton, told Shereé that Porsha told her she couldn't trust any of the girls in the group, including Shereé. That pissed off Shereé because she felt she had been the only girl of the group to have her back.

"Porsha, you know that we've established a little something," Shereé said to Porsha in front of Shamea, Kim and Porsha's sister, Lauren. "Can you honestly say I have not been instrumental in trying to help you get with these girls? Make up with these girls? Have I done something to you to make you say not to trust Shereé?"

Porsha had no idea what Shereé was talking about, so Shamea explained what she had told Shereé. After trying to get an answer out of Porsha, Shereé gave up. That's when Lauren came to the defense of her sister and Kim came to the defense of Shereé. It took about two whole seconds for the situation to go from a conversation to a screaming match.

"OK, that's fine, but I'm talking," Lauren replied.

"Well, you shouldn't be," Shereé fired back.

It wasn't until Lauren started waving her hand in Shereé's face that Kim stepped in, which Porsha quickly shot down, saying Kim's character was "questionable."

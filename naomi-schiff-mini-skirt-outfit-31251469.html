Sky Sports F1's Naomi Schiff 'slays' in stylish boots and mini-skirt outfit loved by fans

July 30, 2024
blog

Sky Sports F1 pundit Naomi Schiff wowed fans with her fashionable outfit during the Austin Grand Prix weekend.

Schiff, 29, is a former W Series driver and was formerly appointed as the diversity and inclusion ambassador for the competition until it folded in 2022. She has since been a regular on Sky Sports’ F1 coverage and appeared again at the United States Grand Prix weekend.

She presented the coverage of Saturday’s sprint shootout in Austin, which saw Max Verstappen take pole and convert his start into victory in the sprint later the same day. Schiff appeared wearing a baby blue mini skirt with blazer to match and boots.

Schiff’s outfit drew plenty of attention from fans on social media, one fan said: "Wow she slaying in that outfit."

What did you make of Naomi Schiff’s outfit? Let us know in the comments section.

Schiff is no stranger to showcasing her fashion as she regularly posts snaps in the F1 paddock, previously wearing figuring-hugging outfits. She also showed off her tats as she posed in a bikini on holiday.

Born in Belgium, Schiff is part Rwandan and grew up in South Africa. She now lives in Paris when she’s not touring around the globe covering F1. She’s also the co-host of Channel 4 car programme Bangers: Cars of the People, alongside rapper Tinie Tempah.

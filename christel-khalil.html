Career and Professional Life

Christel Khalil made her acting debut as a child artist in a 1993 episode of the Fox Channel American television sitcom series ‘The Sinbad Show,’ featuring comedian Sinbad, in the guest role of Crystal.

Other cameo appearances in single episodes of the series ‘Family Matters’ (1996) and ‘George & Leo’ (1997) followed.

In the meanwhile, she played supporting parts in the films, ‘Dragon Fury’ (1995) and ‘Matilda’ (1996).

She played Diana in the short film “You’re Invited to Mary-Kate and Ashley’s Christmas Party” in 1997.

She played Young Claire Mullins in the horror film ‘Milo,’ which was released on October 6, 1998, while Jennifer Jostyn played the elder version of the character.

After that, in 2002, she rose to fame as a performer after taking on the role of Lily Ashby in the American TV soap opera The Young and the Restless. Moreover, she was also featured in the television series ‘2 Broke Girls.’

Moreover, In 2004, she received the main voice part in the French-American animated television series ‘W.I.T.C.H.’ as Cornelia Hale.

As guest appearances, she has appearances on successful series including ‘Malcolm in the Middle’, (2006), ‘And NCIS: Los Angeles’ (2012), among others.

She starred as Christel in the Erik Blanc-directed drama film ‘Chronologia Human’ and as Breanna Wilkinson in the short film ‘Bedlam’ in 2017.

Christel played Lauren opposite Haley Webb and Haley Pullos in Craig Goldsmith’s drama thriller film ‘Good Deed,’ which was released on October 10, 2018.

She appeared in the music video for artist Johnny Britt’s song “Beautiful Queen” in 2012.

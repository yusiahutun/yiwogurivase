Steven Lee Hall Jr's bio

The TV personality was born on 28 March 1983 (age 40 as of 2023) in Amarillo, Texas, United States. He is the son of Steven Hall Sr. (father) and Debbie Hall (mother). Steven Lee Hall Jr's family moved from Texas when he was nine and resettled in Orlando, Florida.

Steven Hall attended Edgewater High School, a public high school in the College Park section of Orlando, Florida. While he was young, Steven’s father and grandfather taught him survival skills.

He did not know that the skills his father and grandpa taught would come in handy many years later. Steven lived in Orlando, Florida, for over 20 years. In 2016, he got the opportunity to join a reality TV show titled Naked and Afraid.

Professional career

Steven Hall did not become a survivalist overnight. Instead, he left Texas and went to Orlando to work as a bartender. He worked for several bars in Orlando until the mid-2010s. In 2016, he became a reality TV personality when he appeared in Naked and Afraid.

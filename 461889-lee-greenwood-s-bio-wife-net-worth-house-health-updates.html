Career

When Greenwood was discovered by Larry McFadden of Mel Tillis' band in 1979, who later became his manager, his career advanced significantly. It Turns Me Inside Out, his debut song, peaked at number 20 in the US country charts in 1981.

Several other singles followed, including two number one songs, Somebody's Gonna Love You and Going, Going, Gone. He collaborated with Barbara Mandrell on an album in 1984, and their song To Me reached the US country charts.

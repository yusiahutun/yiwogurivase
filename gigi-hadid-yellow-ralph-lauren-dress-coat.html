Gigi Hadid Wears Yellow Ralph Lauren Outfit in London

July 15, 2024
blog

Gigi Hadid knows how to work the monochrome trend, and tonight she upgraded it with some extra wattage. The model stepped out for her Gigi Hadid x Maybelline launch party in London wearing a yellow-on-yellow ensemble from Ralph Lauren. The look made for a bright statement and a stark contrast to the British city's cloudy skies.

Hadid's coat and sequined mini dress were from Lauren's Fall 2017 collection, which just premiered during NYFW in the designer's garage. She paired the look with gold, glittery heeled pumps. All together, the look was a kirakira addict's dream come true.

Hadid, 22, is currently celebrating the launch of her cosmetics collaboration with the American beauty giant, which she announced in early October on Instagram. After working on the range for "a year," the wait and hard work paid off. Her debut product for the collection, the Jetsetter Palette, sold out in under 90 minutes.

The product was inspired by Hadid's own inflight makeup kit, and includes neutral eyeshadows, concealer, lip gloss, contouring powder, highlighter, and blusher stripes, as well as a miniature mascara. We can see why beauty fans wanted to get their hands on it so quickly.

Never stop slaying, Gigi.

September 26, 2024

The days when consumers used to wait for their electricity bills to be sent to them via their postal address are over. The Kenya Power and Lighting Company introduced the Kenya Power self-service module that has brought about a tremendous improvement in service delivery. Nowadays, you can conveniently check KPLC bill via USSD, SMS, and e-mail.

You do not have to stay in the dark. The power provider has clear KPLC bill query guidelines to help you get your answers in minutes, if not seconds.

KPLC bill query via SMS - check KPLC bill via text

Kenya Power bill enquiry via SMS relieves you from a great deal of hassle that would leave you in abrupt darkness. Here are the simple steps:

  • Open your phone messages then create a new message
  • Key in the first section of the account number. For instance, if yours reads 123456-98, you’ll type 123456 and exclude the rest

KPLC bill via USSD code

You can also check your postpaid bill and access a wide variety of KPLC services fast and easy by dialling the USSD code *977#. Here, you’ll access prepaid services like buying your token or checking your latest token refill.

You can also report power failure and access KPLC postpaid services such as Pay Bill, My Bill, bill alerts, and Self Reading.

The USSD code also makes it easy to check KPLC bill and manage both your postpaid and prepaid accounts. It also offers the Jua For Sure service for both staff and contractors.

To boost their delivery and enhance the customer experience, KPLC established the e-bill service for efficient checking of balances and electricity bills. This approach allows you to access this information through your e-mail address.
